In the enormous galaxy, there are many stars facing the crisis of overpopulation. For example, the Alexander Empire is typical nation. To resolve this problem, Alexander is launching 4 fleets and looking for the heaven in the legend: 'The Earth'.  Thunder Shooting is a side-scrolling space shooter. The player controls an advanced starfighter, blasting through waves of enemy ships and asteroids. Numerous power-ups appear throughout each stage, which will upgrade or change the player's weaponry, or alter the behavior of the ship's options. At the end of each stage, there is a boss that must be destroyed. It is possible to choose from any of the four stages in order.
