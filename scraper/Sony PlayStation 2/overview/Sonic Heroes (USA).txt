 Sonic and the gang, are back in the latest game, SONIC HEROES. Coming out on the Gamecube and for the first time on Playstation 2,  Xbox And PC! Sonic Heroes has a twist to the gameplay, you choose a team of 3 characters. With different strengths.  There are 4 teams: TEAM SONIC ~ Sonic, Tails and Knuckles TEAM DARK ~ Shadow, Rouge and E-123 Omega TEAM CHAOTIX ~ Espio, Vector, and Charmy Bee TEAM ROSE ~  Amy, Big, and Cream with her Chao sidekick Cheese. There are 4 modes, Story mode, Challenge mode, the Tutorial modes and the new and improved battle mode for multiplayer fun, and there are plenty of secrets to unlock.
