 This updated version of Supercross 2000 lets the players take to 25 different dirt bike tracks, each with their own twists, turns, bumps and jumps while performing insane gravity-defying tricks.  Players can customize their bikes to their liking as well as choose from ten professional riders including the game's namesake, Jeremy McGrath.  There is also multiplayer capabilities that allows to friends to compete head-to-head through split-screen.
