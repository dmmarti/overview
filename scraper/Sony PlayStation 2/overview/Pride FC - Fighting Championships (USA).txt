 All fighting styles are accepted in PRIDE fight. Fight in all positions; standing, in a clinch, and on the ground. 25 fighters from around the world are available to use.  A 16- man single- elimination tournament is available. Play alone, or with up to 16 human players, or just watch 16 computer players fight the tournament. Customize your own character and fight in One Match mode to get ready for the Grad Prix. Also, get your name in one of the Top Ten Fighting Legends by defeating all 25 PRIDE fighters in Survival Mode.
