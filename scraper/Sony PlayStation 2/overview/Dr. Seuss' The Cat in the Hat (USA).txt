 Based on Dr Seuss's Cat in the Hat, you play has the cheeky cat. There are all the original characters including, Conrad, Sally, and Mr. Quinn who tricked Conrad, and Sally into opening the box the Cat was in, as he was released into the outside world. The Cat must help Conrad and Sally to return the magic to the box the cat came in, and get all three pieces of the "Crab Lock" in order to lock the box for good. The game allows to the see the rooms that where not included in the movie.
