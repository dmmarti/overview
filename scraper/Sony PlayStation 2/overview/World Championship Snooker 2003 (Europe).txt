 The 2003 edition of the most comprehensive snooker game series ever features more than 25 top professional players all modelled in high detail - including every world champion from the last 11 years. You can create your own character and compete in all the major tournaments in Career Mode, earning ranking points and unlocking new features as you progress. You can also play 8 or 9-ball Pool, a Coaching Mode to show you the ropes, several fun games and a Trickshot Challenge presented by John Virgo, who commentates in-game alongside Dennis Taylor.
