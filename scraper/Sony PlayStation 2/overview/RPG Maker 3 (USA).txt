 RPG Maker 3 is the third in a series of RPG Maker games (programs) developed for the Sony Playstation.  RPG Maker 3 is a game creation tool for aspiring game designers.  RPG Maker 3 allows its users to create 3D environments, layout dungeons and town, and set up battles.  Players of RPG Maker 3 will enjoy building elaborate worlds and stories in a 3D environment.  Players also have the option of saving their game on to memory cards and sharing their game with friends.
