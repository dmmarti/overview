 Oh no, Spa Town is in trouble! All its animals are sicker than parrots now that's really sick! Luckily, the famous Doctor Dolittle has just opened his new animal hospital and he wants YOU to help him run it. Will you accept the challenge of curing Spa Town's poorly pets? Welcome To Spa Town! - Your new home has a zoo-full of sick animals just itching to see you (some of them literally!), including dogs, cats, giraffes, elephants and even tigers! •Talk To Animals! - Use Dr Dolittle's powers to chat to animals and discover what ails them! •Find The Cure! - Use your mixing machine to combine the ingredients for special medicines! •It's An Emergency! - Listen out for your mobile you never know when an animal might need urgent help!
