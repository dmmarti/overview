 In Adiboo and the Energy Thieves, Adiboo and your child have to stop a mysterious robot from devouring the planet's natural resources of water, lava, rocks and water. The game features over 14 levels of enthralling game play including platform levels, breathtaking races and duals. A multiplayer mode plus time trial sequences test every kid's limits. The game also features unique personalised gameplay which analyses the child's ability and automatically adapts the difficulty of the game play. In this way both inexperienced children and confirmed players can enjoy the pleasure of console gaming.
