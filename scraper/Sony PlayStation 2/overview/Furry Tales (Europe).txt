 After a thousand years of warfare, the conflict still rages between sheep and wolves. This conflict, whose foundations have now been forgotten by new generations, devastates meadows and forests, soiling them with flock or pack blood.  Wolves, jealous of green pastures where sheep nonchalantly graze, dedicate them ferocious hatred and want to prove their superiority in the food chain. Bored with being part of the "weak" animals, the sheep have decided to react, with the help of weapons they are ready to proudly defend their wool.
