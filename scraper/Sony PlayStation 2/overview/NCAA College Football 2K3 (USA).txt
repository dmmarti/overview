 This is Sega Sports' collegiate football sim including brand-new player models, completely redesigned stadiums, all 25 official Bowl Championship Series (BCS) bowls, and a revamped Legacy mode in which gamers can recruit high-school stars and compare lifetime records against other collegiate programs in the historical rankings system. Using the game's Legacy mode, you will be able to follow the entire career of a college player from freshman year to entering the NFL draft.
