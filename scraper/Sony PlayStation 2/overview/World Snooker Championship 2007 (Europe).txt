 World Snooker Championship 2007 continues the World Championship Snooker line of games. The game recreates 20 real-life snooker tournaments. Players can create their own character and compete against (or play as) professional snooker players such as: Shaun Murphy, Ronnie O'Sullivan, Efren Reyes and Earl Strickland. Game modes include Snooker, 8 Ball, 9 Ball and Trick-shot. This latest edition includes improvements on the visual aids the player can turn on. The player is now able to see where the cue ball will end up after his shot.  In-game commentary is provided by John Parrot, John Virgo and Steve Davis.
