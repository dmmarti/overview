 Based on the familiar Habitrail brand of pet accessories and its character, Herbie the Hamster, players will get to to navigate Herbie safely around the house inside his plastic exercise ball. Explore and solve puzzles in four environments, including a kid's bedroom, the kitchen, the garden and even the attic. There are even 5 easier levels for junior players as well as a 'Beginner' and 'Expert' mode for those with nimble fingers!
