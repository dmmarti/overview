 Supercar Street Challenge puts you behind the wheels of nine of the hottest cars on the planet, like the Saleen S7 and Zonda C-12S. Race on 23 courses in 7 cities in multiple game modes. Even modify your supercar in the unique Steve Saleen design studio.   The game is essentially a Need for Speed clone with all the same structures built in, including multiple classes of cars, lots of courses and exotic cars.
