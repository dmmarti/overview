 This latest update to the NFL Blitz series offers more options and refines the options presented earlier in the series.  Of significant note is the create a player option.  By desiging the look of the player as well as assigning various stats to their abilities, you can craft a whole team to compete in the league.  Also improved is the artificial intelligence in the one-player game setting.  Fantasy Stadiums in exotic locales and Fantasy Players are another new option.  Updated rosters, schedules, uniforms, and stadiums including the addition of the Houston Texans.
