 In International Golf Pro you can play a tournament over 3 different courses - England, Morocco and California, each with 18 holes. There are 3 Different modes of play - Quick Round, Match Play, or Championship mode on top of the Practice Mode. The game has a small selection of semi-famous golfing personalities. It uses the standard Swingometer for the golf swings, where the power and hook/slice is set by your timing and reaction speed.
