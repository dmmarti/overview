 Cart Racer based on the second Flintstones Movie. Eight racetracks, can also be raced mirrored or reverse. 9 different vehicles (not counting the bonus vehicle - a UFO controlled by Gazoo the Great). 4 Player multiplayer split screen mode. Diverse weapons and power ups. Several drivers to choose from (Barney, Fred, Wilma, Chip Rockefella etc.). A multitude of different game modes such as bomb hunt, where the player needs to pass along a bomb before it explodes.
