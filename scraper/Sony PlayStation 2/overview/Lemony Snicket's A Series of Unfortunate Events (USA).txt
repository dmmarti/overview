 Life for the Baudelaire orphans is incredibly unpredictable and rather unfortunate.  After losing their parents in a mysterious fire, the orphans are sent to live with their sinister uncle, Count Olaf, who will stop at nothing to get his greedy hands on the Baudelaire famile fortune!  Only by combining their talents will the orphans have a chance to defeat the evil Count Olaf and his horrible henchmen.  - Play as all three Baudelaire orphans, each with their own unique skills.  - Adventure through 16 action-packed missions based on the movie and book series.  - Build gadgets to help you defeat Count Olaf and his horrendous henchmen.
