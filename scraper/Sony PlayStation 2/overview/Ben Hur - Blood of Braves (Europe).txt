 Microids' chariot racing game for the Sony PlayStation 2 will let you assume the role of a number of fabled heroes from ancient Rome. 15 characters to choose from • Master 64 spells, heal, magic attack and skill upgrades • Engage in close combat, or long range battles • 17 Mythical circuits: Olympus, Pompeii, the Circus Maximus... • Career, Arcade, Time Attack, Deathmatch and Multiplayer game modes.
