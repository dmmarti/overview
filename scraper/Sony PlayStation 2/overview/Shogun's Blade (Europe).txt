 Re-live the historic battles of Mediaeval Japan with the Warlords, the feared "Shogun". Guide the young Samurai warriors through the bitter struggles for the destiny of the land. Change the course of history! The game zone is crawling with dozens of fighters, cut a path through using devastating techniques to defeat the Supreme Commander, Tokugawa Ieyasu. Formidable lock-on system. Use the lock-on to target numerous enemies and attack them in one hit using the powerful Lightning Strike. You can target up to 100 enemies simultaneously!
