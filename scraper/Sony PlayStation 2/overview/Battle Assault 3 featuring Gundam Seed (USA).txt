 Cosmic Era 71. Tensions between the Earth Alliance and ZAFT finally reach a breaking point, culmnulating in the outbreak of the Bloody Valentine War. Overpowered by ZAFT's revolutionary Mobile Suits, the Earth Alliance has built their own suits with brand new technologies. However, five ZAFT soldiers initiated a daring and brazen theft of the suits, leading to one young boy, Kira Yamato, to encounter the machine he'd call "Gundam".
