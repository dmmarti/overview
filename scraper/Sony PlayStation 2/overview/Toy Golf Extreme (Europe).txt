 Toy Golf, the crazy golf game with a twist, is back for another round of bogeys and birdies. Toy Golf Extreme will have you putting for eagles all over the house as it takes crazy golf indoors making for the ideal casual gaming experience.  Toy Golf Extreme lets you putt your way through courses in house hold locations such as the Garage, Living Room, Kitchen, Bedroom and Attic with a total of 45 holes to try to master.
