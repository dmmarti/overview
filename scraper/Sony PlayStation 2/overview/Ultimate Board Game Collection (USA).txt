 Ultimate Board Game Collection includes over 20 classic board and tabletop games. Each game contains a trophy that can be unlocked by completing a specific goal. Depending on the game, up to six players can compete by passing around a single controller.  The games include:  1. 3D Tic-Tac-Toe 2. Anagrams 3. Backgammon 4. Checkers 5. Chess 6. Chinese Checkers 7. Concentration 8. Dice 9. Dominoes 10. Enigma 11. Go 12. Jigsaws 13. Mahjong Solitaire 14. Mancala 15. Naval Battle 16. Parcheesi 17. Quatro 18. Reversi 19. Snakes and Ladders 20. Word Cubes
