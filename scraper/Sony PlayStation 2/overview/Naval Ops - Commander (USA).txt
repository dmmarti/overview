 Naval Ops: Commander -- In this game, you are part of the Tactical Battle Group, the last defense against the agression of a Virshia. Through Virshia's aggressive assault on the world's oceans, nations fell and others still hang in the balance. As a commander, your job is to lead missions against the Virshian forces. Use your knowledge of ships and naval warfare to lead the free nations to victory - and save the world from conquest. New bird's-eye view and user-controlled camera - sweep each sector for surface, air and land-based threats
