 Crazy Golf lets you play the game like never before - the game comes packed with mini courses filled with hazards and obstacles that will test your putting skills to the limit. The custom physics system allows your ball to ricochet, bounce and bobble with stunning realism.  The game is set in three unique environments; underwater, up in the sky, and finally in outer space. The aim of the game is to win all the trophies. To succeed you will need to become a great Crazy Golfer, and beat the target scores throughout the game. Only the very greatest players will be able to win all the Gold Trophies
