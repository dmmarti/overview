 Embark on an adventure through the lands of ancient Egypt in the latest installment of the Luxor series. Enjoy 88 levels of graphics portraying pyramids, temples and many more of Egypt's marvels. Guide your mystical winged-shooter, swooping back and forth, as you destroy magical spheres before they reach the pyramid lying at the end of their path. To help eliminate enemy spheres, Luxor: Pharaoh's Challenge features more explosive power-ups than before.
