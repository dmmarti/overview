 Take control of more than two tons of racing power, hurtling along the track at 150mph! Truck Racing 2 gives you all the action of racing these massive machines, combining intuitive handling with astounding realism. You'll have to race hard in order to beat aggressive opponents who won't hesitate to use all of their bulk to shunt you off your line. If you think you can control 1100 horsepower of speeding rig then jump into the driver's seat and get ready for Truck Racing 2; the ultimate driving experience.
