 Spy Hunter 2 is a fast paced action racing game that features the innovative ability to transform into 3 different vehicles which, allows you to navigate through 16 diverse environments such as ragged snow covered mountain ranges, tropical swamps, and rugged dirt and freeway roads. Armed with an upgradeable arsenal of futuristic weaponry you are on a quest to end the terrorist organization called “Nostra”. As you battle through various AI controlled enemy the Spy Hunter 2 story unfolds revealing more in-depth information about the new Spy Hunter 2 vehicle and Alec Sects connection with his mysterious female co-agent.
