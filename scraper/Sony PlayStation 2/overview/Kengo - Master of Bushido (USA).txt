 Kengo: Master of Bushido is a Fighting game in which you try to master "The Way of the Warrior" (Bushido). The game features three modes single-player, versus and tournament. The first starts your training where your master teaches you all the knowledge required for this martial art form. In your Dojo you will slowly advance in technique and weaponry, starting with wooden sticks and ending with katanas. Your character has several statistics such as strength and speed which can be upgraded in RPG-like fashion. Besides fighting you will also learn to meditate in various ways. In versus or tournament mode you can bring your experience into practice and play against friends or the computer.
