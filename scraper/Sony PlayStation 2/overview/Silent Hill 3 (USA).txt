 In "Silent Hill 3", you play Heather, a simple teenage girl. One fine day she goes shopping, but suddenly finds herself trapped in a strange, terrifying place - the eerie town Silent Hill. She hears footsteps...Disgusting, creepy monsters attack her, and she has to defend herself! Exploring Silent Hill - and her own past - Heather must find a way to escape the dreadful town.  This is a sequel to two famous survival horror games. The gameplay features real-time combat and puzzle-solving.
