 This is the latest installment in the Wipeout franchise. Basically the game hasn't changed much. The player can fly with a total of 8 vehicles and teams. The teams are: Xios (Finland), Feisar (European Consortium), Van Uber (Germany), Auricom (USA and Canada), Tigron (Russia), G-tech (Japan), EG-R (China) and Pirhana (Brazil).  This time the tracks also contain loops. And as usual the player can pick up numerous kinds of weapons along the tracks, and this time every ship has its special weapon
