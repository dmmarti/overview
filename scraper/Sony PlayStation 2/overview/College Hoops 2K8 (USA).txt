 Standard update of the long-running 2K series, with some added new features.  One of which is the 6th man advantage that measures the crowds energy and excitement when scoring often and playing good defense, causing the meter to rise, the higher the meter, the better your players will become and demoralize the opposing team.  Another new feature is the ability to train your players with former college players such as Mike Conley Jr. of Ohio State, Julian Wright of Kansas, Glen Davis of LSU, Alando Tucker of Wisconsin, Al Thornton of Florida State, J.J. Redick of Duke, and Greg Oden of Ohio State.  Also has online capability that lets you share your rosters, legacies and game settings with other players online and lets you play in season and tournament modes as well.
