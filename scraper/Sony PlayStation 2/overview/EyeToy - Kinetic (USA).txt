 The Personal Fitness Trainer EyeToy: Kinetic is a workout game that puts the player up on the screen.  Over 20 exercises cover four fitness zones: combat, cardio, toning, and mind and body.  Developed with Nike Motionworks, Kinetic's background includes aerobics, martial arts, and dance.  Players can customize a 12-week training program and the EyeToy technology analyzes their actions and provides feedback on their performance.  Up to four players can take turns with each routine and have their progress saved.  An EyeToy USB Camera is included in the package.
