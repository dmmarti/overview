 Ra.One: The Game is an action game that features characters from the 2011 Indian science fiction superhero film Ra.One. It is a prequel to the movie and has many characters from it. There are eleven playable characters that fight one-on-one or one against many in 20 3D arenas. They fights by combining melee attacks into combos, but can also use ranged weapons. The environment can be used in various ways and there are height differences. There are three game modes: Story mode, Brawl mode and Challenge mode. Multiplayer is supported for up to four players.
