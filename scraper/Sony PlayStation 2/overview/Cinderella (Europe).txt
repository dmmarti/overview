 In the suburbs of the city of Rushton, live a beautiful young girl who is kept hidden and busy with household chores by her lazy step mother and her two horrible daughters. One beautiful hot day the Princes messenger announces that the Prince is inviting all the citizens the royal ball that very night. The step mother and her daughters force the girl to wait on them as they prepare theselves and leave for the ball. The young girl is left behind unhappy and exhausted. Suddenly her fairy god mother appears. Will Cinderella get to go to the ball?
