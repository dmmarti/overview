 RTL Ski Jumping 2003 is a ski jumping game with a hot-seat multiplayer mode. The jumpers have to built up based on statistics similar to a role-playing game. They are trained and managed through the ski jumping season 2002/2003. As a sequel to RTL Skispringen 2002, it includes a number of new features compared to the previous iteration. There are now different leagues (Junior, Amateur, Pro), it runs on a new graphics engine, multiplayer games are supported through internet, LAN or hot-seat, there is a new commentator, a transfer market for trainers, and a sports quiz with more than 500 questions.
