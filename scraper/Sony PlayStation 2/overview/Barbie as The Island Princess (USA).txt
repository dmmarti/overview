 Barbie as The Island Princess follows the storyline of the popular DVD launch in a fun party-style format. You`ll play as Rosella, the shipwrecked princess, who is raised by a family of animal friends and rescued by the adventurous Prince Antonio from her tropical island. Through a series of minigames coined by music and characters from the movie, you will venture from Rosella`s island home to the Prince`s coastal castle in a fun filled adventure!
