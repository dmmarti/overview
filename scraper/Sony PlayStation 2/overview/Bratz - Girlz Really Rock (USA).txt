 The Bratz are out of Stylesville and attending Camp Starshine, the summer camp for the most talented students of the performing arts. They meet Anna, a lonely ballerina who has dedicated so much of her life to dance that she has excluded everything else - including fun and friendship. The girls befriend Anna and help her to be a more rounded individual. When she has a crisis of confidence, her new friends are there to rally around her, helping her create a performance for the end of camp showcase that no one will forge
