 Embark on an amazing summer adventure at the Roberts Stables. While learning to groom, feed, and care for your horse, you'll take on riding lessons across five different equestrian disciplines. Explore a vast island, undertake time trials and join in fun tasks like gathering blueberries for a slumber party at the bunkhouse.You'll train, ride and jump up to five different horse breeds, help out the local islanders with their chores, unlock stylish new riding outfits, and earn medals and ribbons for outstanding performances.
