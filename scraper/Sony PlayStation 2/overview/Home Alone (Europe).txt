 Your parents are away and the burglars are at the door! It's time to bring out the crazy home-made tricks and traps in this thrilling action game of the hilarious Home Alone movie from Twentieth Century Fox. From the garden to the attic, and the basement to the bedrooms, use your wits and a couple of well-placed firecrackers to bash the bad guys through 10 levels of fantastic Home Alone action.
