 SEGA SuperStars is a game developed by Sonic Team for the PlayStation 2 EyeToy USB Camera. It features a series of mini-games based on notable SEGA franchises. The characters are controlled by waving your hands.  1. Billy Hatcher and the Giant Egg 2. Crazy Taxi 3. ChuChu Rocket! 4. House of the Dead 5. NiGHTS into Dreams 6. Puyo Pop Fever 7. Samba de Amigo 8. Sonic the Hedgehog 9. Space Channel 5 10. Super Monkey Ball 11. Virtua Fighter 12. Virtua Striker
