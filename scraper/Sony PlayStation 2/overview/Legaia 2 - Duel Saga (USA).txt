 A magical crystal that supplies infinite water to the small village of Nohl is stolen by force, later causing a solar eclipse that drowns the land in an evil light. The monsters flourish and all seems lost. Now Lang, a rookie member of the Vigilance Corps, must fight his way across uncharted lands to recover the crystal and restore peace to the land.  Master the Tactical Arts System: a unique fighting ability that enables you to unlock special moves in the midst of turn-based combat.  Call on elemental forces to battle enemies, heal injured allies and destroy barriers.  Combine weapons and items to create new and more powerful weapons and equipment.
