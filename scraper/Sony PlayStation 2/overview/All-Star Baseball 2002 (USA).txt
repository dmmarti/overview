 All-Star Baseball 2002 has the complete 2001-2002 MLBPA rosters.  ASB2002 features many different game modes, including Season, Franchise, and Tournament.  You may choose to play as one of the current teams, or you can go back to the past and play as people like Nolan Ryan or Reggie Jackson.  ASB2002 features a create mode that allows you to create your own player and/or team.
