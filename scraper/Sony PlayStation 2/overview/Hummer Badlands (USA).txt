 Get down and dirty in this hummer racing game. Drive and choose from 19 different hummer models, as you race to win in different races through all the modes of the game: Championship, Tug'O'war, and Extreme Off-road where you race to the top of a mountain through intense weather over fallen trees, bushes and tall grass. As you win, you unlock custom hummers, tracks, and customizations to upgrade your vehicle. Multiplayer includes 2-player head-to-head races on split-screen.
