 Years ago,  Lord Morgan Hawkmoor and his army battled a deadly enemy powered by neverending souls. The only way they could be defeated was to seal them inside a vault, hoping it would never be released.  However, in the time of the great hero Maximo, someone has let the great evil loose from its cell, and its up to our heart boxer wearing soldier to defeat it once and for all. After searching with out reward for his lost love Sophia, Maximo must concentrate all his power towards the evil that awaits.  Set just a few months after the events of the PS2 hit, Maximo: Ghosts to Glory, you must take Maximo through the hell ridden roads, using his sword and shield to battle through the great many creatures that await him. But be careful, much like his last adventure, Maximo can lose his armour, leaving him open to attack.
