 In Portal Runner you assume the role of the bow and arrow wielding Vikki G. Thinking she was investigating a hot story Vikki is lured into a diabolical trap set by her nemesis, the beautiful Blue super spy Brigitte Bleu. This trap sends Vikki to mysterious land where strange creatures lurk. Along the way, Vikki stumbles upon a Lion named Leo, and together they travel from portal to portal hoping that the next portal will lead them home.
