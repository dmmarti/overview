 X Squad is a 3rd person arcade-like squad-based shooter. It's a conversion of the Electronic Arts/Square Japan title X-Fire released on the PS2 3 months earlier.   The US version has additional features such as:   3 Difficulty levels   A new story line   Redone characters   Improved AI difficulty scaling  In the year 2037, Ash leads a small special forces group to discover the mystery that lies behind the loss in communication from a secret military complex.
