 AN EXHILARATING ACTION GAME WITH A CRUEL BUT SOFT-HEARTED MAIN CHARACTER The wizard Jagodd has conquered the village and created an army of followers using an ancient forbidden art. Only one lone scarecrow did not yield to his wishes! The scarecrow can use the power of his punches or a pair of chainsaws to get rid of any obstacle. At the end of each of the seven levels humongous bosses are waiting for you! Once a game is over, ther is still lots of fun in store!
