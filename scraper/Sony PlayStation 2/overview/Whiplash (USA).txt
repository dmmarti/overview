 Chained together and doomed for destruction, you must make your escape from the evil animal testing laboratory. As Spanx, the only weapon and tool you have is the indestructible Redmond  Dip Redmond in toxic waste to poison enemies, electro-charge him to zap attackers, fill him with helium to lift you to higher platforms.  Make your getaway, free other animals and sabotage the twisted corporation.
