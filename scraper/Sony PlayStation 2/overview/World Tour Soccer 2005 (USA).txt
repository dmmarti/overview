 WTS 2005 is 989 Sports perennial soccer title dating back to 2002. This one features over 900 International teams to choose from as well as over 18,000 different players from 30 different divisions and 22 leagues. It also as classic teams to choose from such as 90's Barcelona Team, 80's Milan team and the 70's Liverpool Team.  There are four modes to compete in: Exhibition, Season, Career and Challenge as well as competing in World and Euro Cups. It has improved graphics, an updated trading system, a revamped control scheme from its previous incarnation and new updated stadiums.
