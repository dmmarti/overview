 Panzer Front Ausf. B is a tank simulation game, in which the player takes control of a squad of tanks from either German, Italian or British armies.  The game is about the second World War and happens in the African Front. Gameplay consists of realistic missions in which there are some objectives to complete such as destroying a target, moving from point A to B and so forth. There are a number of different tanks to choose from, and each have accurate damage modeling. Panzer Front Ausf. B is a strange case in console world as there isn't so many realistic simulations available on them.
