 Winter Sports: The Ultimate Challenge is an arcade-oriented 3D simulation of the most popular winter sports. It features 15 different disciplines in 9 different sports like skiing, bobsleigh, speedskating, ski jumping, figure skating and curling. In campaign mode you can try to master all of the 42 challenges to unlock more levels. The competition mode offers an Olympic event with opening and medal ceremony. The realism is enhanced through commentators, TV cameras, mascots and background sound. There are multiplayer modes for up to four players.
