 Pro Evolution Soccer 2009 is the eight game of the Pro Evolution Soccer Series and features for the first time the "Become A Legend" mode in which you can create your own player and improve him playing in clubs all over Europe as well as playing with your national time in many international cups.  The Ligue 1 and the Eredivise are the fully licensed leagues. The Premier League, the LFP and the Calcio leagues are partially licensed and for the first time you'll be able to play the UEFA Champions League. You can also play with many other teams from other countries as well as 60 national teams in many leagues and cups. The "Master League" mode is still available.  The game has new moves, readjustment for ball movements, new animations and improved graphics.
