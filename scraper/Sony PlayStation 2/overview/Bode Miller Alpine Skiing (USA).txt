 As Bode Miller, skiing superstar, you will feel at home on just about any slope. Take on the Kandahar ski-run or put your skills to the test on Saslong, the demanding World Cup course in Groden. Bode Miller Alpine Skiing offers a finely tuned balance of role playing and racing action, enabling you to build your skills, wealth and equipment as you descend upon the pistes of the world's greatest ski venues.
