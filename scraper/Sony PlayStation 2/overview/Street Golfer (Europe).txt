 Street Golfer is a golf game which looks at golf from a different perspective.  The streets have become the golf courses, the rules have remained the same.  The best golfers have decided to join the world in the event and flew to the city selected.  Here you have to fight for the first place.  You can choose your favorite character and thus an innovative and strange game of golf.  Be aware of the rules, because they are just as stringent as in the real sport.
