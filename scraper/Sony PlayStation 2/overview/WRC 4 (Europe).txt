 The 4th game of the WRC series, WRC 4: The Official Game of the FIA World Rally Championship contains all the official races, drivers and cars of the 2004 season.  The player can compete in a championship mode, a quick race mode, and a time trial mode. Players can also play the Pro Driver Challenge in which players can experience a career in WRC as they race through the key events in a driver's journey from beginner to the top. Also, a new type of race appears called the Super Special. This unique rally event pits the player against another rally car in a two-lane rally across the super special stage.
