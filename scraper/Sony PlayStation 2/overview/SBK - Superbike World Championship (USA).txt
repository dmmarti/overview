 SBK - Superbike World Championship  is the official videogame of the Superbike World Championship.  It offers a wide range of gameplay, selectable from 5 different pre-sets, from a very basic gameplay for new players to a full featured simulation for those looking for the challenge of riding a Superbike.  The player has the possibility to choose from the 2008 official riders, teams and tracks, but also improve the performances of his bike, managing and tuning a lot of aspects like top speed, gear ratios, suspension stiffness, and set of tires for single race.
