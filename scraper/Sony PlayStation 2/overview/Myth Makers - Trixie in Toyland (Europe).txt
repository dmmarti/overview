 Myth Makers: Trixie in Toyland is a 3D platform game in a fantasy toys world.  Evil Penumbra has stolen the Myth Makers Orbs and has hidden them in Toyland. The player controls young Trixie from a third-person perspective through levels with platform puzzles and enemies that are taken out by swinging his rattle or locking on to them and shooting energy balls. Trixie must collect eight power rods to activate a teleporter to the next level. Game consists of nine levels + tutorial level.
