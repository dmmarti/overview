Giga Wing 2 is an excellent sequel to 'Giga Wing' featuring 3 types of reflects and amazing 3-D graphics.    The rebellion's savage forces have returned in an all-out campaign for universal domination. Only you can defeat them! Pick your aircraft and prepare for hi-tech dogfights, relentless enemy fire, fantastic power-ups and devastating weapons like the Reflect Laser and Reflect Force. Use the Score Multiplier to rack up astronomical high scores.
