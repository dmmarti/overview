The Survival Kids series continues with this second entry on the DS. Two teenagers are washed ashore on a deserted tropical island when their cruise ship sinks. They must work together to survive and escape from the island.  New features in this game include extreme weather such as hurricanes and earthquakes. The player can now fight the native creatures with weapons they have found or made. This sequel also uses the touchscreen more heavily, and offers 4 player single card DS Download play of the various mini-games.
