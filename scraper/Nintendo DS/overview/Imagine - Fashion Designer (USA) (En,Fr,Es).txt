Become a top designer in a world-famous fashion agency! As a new fashion designer, enter a famous fashion agency in New York. Your goal is to create trendy new designs to become the hippest fashion designer. Succeed in the missions you are given and learn the basic fashion concepts to perform new tasks to achieve your dreams! As you progress through the adventure, you will interact with many fashion specialists and discover many different &amp; glamorous places in New York &amp; abroad!
