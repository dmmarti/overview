Sega Casino features 11 staple casino games that include Blackjack, Roulette, Craps, Baccarat, and Texas Hold 'Em Poker, as well as 7 Card Stud, Video Poker, Keno, and Chuck a Luck as unlockable games. VIP Rooms for higher betting are also unlockable.

There are three modes - Free Mode, where you can play any of the unlocked games regardless of money, Casino Mode, where you play to win certain amounts of money to unlock games, and Wireless Mode, where up to five players can play single card download over local wi-fi. The controls are fully enabled for touch screen play with the stylus.
