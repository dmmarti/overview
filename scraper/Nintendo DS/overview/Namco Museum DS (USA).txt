A compilation of some classic games. The games included:  1. Pac-Man 2. Galaga 3. Xevious 4. Dig Dug II 5. Galaxian 6. Mappy 7. The Tower of Druaga 8. Pac-Man VS.  There is a screen mode selection which allows the use of different screen combinations on the Nintendo DS. There is  also wireless battles in Pac-Man VS, which features downloadable play where four players can battle.
