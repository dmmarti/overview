Call of Duty: World at War from Activision for the Nintendo DS brings WWII gameplay to the palm of your hand. Achieve victory in seven unique mini-games as you confront enemies on European and Pacific battlefields. Gameplay employs the Nintendo DS stylus and dual-screen interface for unique control. Transmit secret messages with Morse Code, disarm mines, parachute to safety, heal fellow soldiers, provide artillery support, and more. Connect online in four-player multiplayer action with more than 10 maps available for Deathmatch, Team Deathmatch, Capture the Flag, and Hunter/Prey.
