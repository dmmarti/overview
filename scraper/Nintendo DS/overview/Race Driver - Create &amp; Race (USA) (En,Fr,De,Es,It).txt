Race Driver: Create &amp; Race on DS is a racing game designed specifically for the small Nintendo notebook. Find 32 circuits, including the famous Silverstone circuit, 25 licensed vehicles (AMG Mercedes C, Chevrolet Silverado Truck) and official drivers (Mika Hakkinen, Jean Alesi, Heinz-Harald Frentzen ...). Create the circuits of your dreams via the publisher and challenge up to 3 of your friends on a local network or via the Nintendo Wi-Fi connection.
