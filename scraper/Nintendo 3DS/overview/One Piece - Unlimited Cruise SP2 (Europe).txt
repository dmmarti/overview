One Piece: Unlimited Cruise SP2 lets players choose from more than 12 of their favourite characters to play as and over 15 boss characters from the original anime series to do battle with.    In this epic adventure, One Piece fans set sail on the seven seas to discover new islands brimming with adventure. When the only choice is to stand and fight, players will pull, turn and swing to battle notorious villains using each characters own unique set of moves, including Luffys 'Gum Gum Storm' and Namis 'Cyclone Tempo'.    The One Piece: Unlimited Cruise SP2 series is the perfect blend of action and charm that fans will find irresistible.
