Ben 10: Galactic Racing is a 3D kart racing game staring characters from the Ben 10  animated series. The player can choose from fifteen characters from the series like Ben or Kevin Levin or one of Ben?s man Alien forms.    The racing is similar to Mario Kart and also has items on the track to help the player as well as speed strips. The following modes are offered: Grand Prix Circuit, Single Race, Time Trials, Short Circuit and Showdowns. A total of twenty-five tracks can be driven that feature five different types of landscapes.
