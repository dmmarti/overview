Take the "adventure of a lifetime" with Doodle Jump Adventures on the Nintendo 3DS with exclusive new content. Journey up, up, up a sheet of graph paper, picking up jet packs, avoiding black holes, and blasting baddies with nose balls along the way. Visit cool locations in the jungle, space, and ninja worlds This game is INSANELY addictive with diabolical boss levels in each world As an added twist, there are collectibles placed throughout the levels to give you another reason to push the Doodler a little higher. Simple controls and great new locations make this game hard to put down.
