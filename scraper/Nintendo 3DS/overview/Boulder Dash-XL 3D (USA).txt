In this the sequel to the 1984 classic action-puzzler, players set forth as Rockford or Crystal and mine their way through a variety of hazardous, yet stunning, three dimensional caves while avoiding crushing boulders and an eerie menagerie of killer creatures.  Features:  - Dazzling 3D Graphics glisten on Nintendo 3DS  - 4 Varied Gaming Worlds (Ice, Ancient, Reactor, Space)  - 5 Game Modes (Arcade, Puzzle, Zen, Score Attack and Retro)  - Over 150 All New and Exciting Caves to Conquer  - New Scoring mechanics provide an exciting challenge for high score hunters.  - Share unlocks and high score with your friends via StreetPass
