Pippi Longstocking for 3DS is a 3D Puzzle/Adventure game based on Astrid Lindgren's wonderful stories about the strongest girl in the world Pippi Longstocking. In the game the player gets the opportunity to meet and help well-known characters, solve problems in Pippis own way and play together with friends in new and exciting adventures. The art style in the game is a digital interpretation of the classical illustrations by Ingrid Nyman.Join Pippi and her friends in an all new adventure!
