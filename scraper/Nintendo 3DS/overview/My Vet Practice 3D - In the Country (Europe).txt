Build beautiful pens and stables for many different kinds of animals.   You decide which animals to treat or to go out on house visits and   examine the animals on the scene. Really sick animals can be admitted   for in- patient care and healed in your animal hospital.          Exciting pet care and building fun in real 3D!        Care for animals like deer, lambs, dogs, cats, bunnies, parakeets and     horses.        Enjoy examinations and treatments with fantastic 3D effects:     magnifying glass, thermometer, ultrasound scan, syringe, bandage,     tweezers and many more.        Innovative 3D-Photo-Feature: Take real 3D pictures of yourself with     the 3DS camera and use them in the game - now you are a veterinarian     for real!        Use the gyroscope controls of your Nintendo 3DS and explore the ride     out route with your own horse.        Visit the stores in the city or explore and upgrade your own vet     practice.        Collect Nintendo Playcoins to change them into ingame currency.
