The Peanuts Movie: Snoopy's Grand Adventure is a side-scrolling platform video game based on The Peanuts Movie. The game launched on November 3, 2015 in North America, and November 6, 2015 in Europe and Australia for the Nintendo 3DS, PlayStation 4, Wii U, Xbox 360, and Xbox One. It was distributed by Fox Digital Entertainment, published by Activision and developed by Behaviour Interactive.    The player plays as Snoopy and can play as Woodstock in co-op to help find Charlie Brown. In order to do this the player must go through several levels, each one ending at Snoopy's Doghouse. Along the way the player will collect Jelly Beans, use various costumes, and find members of the Beagle Scouts.    The player will also be able to customize Snoopy and Woodstock.
