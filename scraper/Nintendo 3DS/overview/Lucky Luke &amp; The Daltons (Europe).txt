Lucky Luke: The Daltons is a collection of mini-games based on the famous comic franchise Lucky Luke and sequel to Go West: A Lucky Luke Adventure.    The Dalton brothers have managed to escape their prison. Using Ma Dalton's map (which was actually upside down), they get to the wrong direction and get lost in the desert. In their hallucination, they see Ma Dalton as she tells them what to do to get out of their hallucination...    There are 6 language players can choose: English, French, Spain, German, Italian and Dutch; three game modes: The Great Escape, Party time and Arcade Mode. Players use the stylus or the control pad for each part of the game, depending on what the game demands. The game supports multi-player with up to four people.    Mini-games include racing, platforming, puzzle, shooting and cards segments among others.
