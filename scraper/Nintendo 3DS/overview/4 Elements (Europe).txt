Collect 16 mysterious cards    Unlock the 4 ancient books of magic  Incredible gameplay    Beyond endless seas, an ancient kingdom is in trouble! A land once full of life has become a lifeless desert. The magic of the four elements that kept it running for centuries has been corrupted by evil powers and the altars no longer yield the energy of life. It is up to you to unlock the 4 ancient books of magic and collect 16 mysterious cards that will help you restore the kingdom to life. An adorable little fairy will be your aide in this perilous journey as you encounter elementals, dragons, knights, and various magic creatures
