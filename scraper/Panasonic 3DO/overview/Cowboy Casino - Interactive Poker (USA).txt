 The player enters a saloon ready to play some cards. The game contains 4 different types of card games to gamble at which are 5 Card Draw, 7 Card Stud, 5 Card Stud, and Texas Hold'em. The player has the option during hands to either call, raise, or fold. If one of the four computer opponents wins a hand, a short FMV clip is played. The games original packaging includes a small paperback "The Basics of Winning Poker" by J. Edward Allen.
