 This enhanced version of the Joe Montana series features 3D models and even full-motion video of Joe Montana himself.  It sports a full NFL license with all 28 teams of the time with their respective nicknames and logos, along with 10 of the all-time greatest Super Bowl teams including '85 Bears, the '72 Dolphins, just to name a few, that the player can compete against if they win the biggest game of the year.  The gameplay is a behind the quarterback view with 3D angles including four different replay options including a "Helmet Cam".  It features digitized voices and videos including Joe Montana in which the player can consult him three times during a game.  The standard modes also apply including regular exhibition games and a full-season mode complete with playoffs and the ever important, Super Bowl.
