Dialide is a space shooter developed and published by Catsoft. The game was announced during the comic market 36 in the Summer of 1989. The object of the game is to reach the end of each level and then defeat the level boss. Your space ship has two firing modes each located on the wing of the craft and they are controlled separately by the A &amp; B buttons. Collected weapons will change the firing mode of one of the wings, depending on which side of the space craft they are collected with.</Notes
