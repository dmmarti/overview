Cavern Raider is a side view vertical scrolling game where the screen scrolls constantly, and your mission is to navigate various caverns, each with five stages to raid them. As you fall downwards you must avoid or bomb various obstacles while making sure you don't touch the sides of the caverns. You are able to move left or right, downwards a short distance and back upwards.If you touch the sides of the caverns or any object then you lose one of five lives.
