Rescue at Rigel is a 1980 science fiction role-playing video game written and published by Automated Simulations (later known as Epyx). It is based on a modified version of their Temple of Apshai game engine, which was used for most of their releases in this era. The game was released for the Apple II, IBM PC (as a PC booter), TRS-80, Commodore PET, VIC-20, and Atari 8-bit family.</Notes
