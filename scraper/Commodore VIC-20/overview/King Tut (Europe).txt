Your task is to guide Archie, the intrepis explorer, around the grim corridors deep within the tomb of king tut to find the sacred, golden death mask. The tomb is heavily protected with Guardians who must be avoided at all costs if you wish to survive. 
 
Your lamp is unreliable and will go out after a short time in the tomb, therefor, you must find the mask and take it to the exit as quickly as possible. 
 
The tomb is filled with gold witch you can collect on the way to boost your score. Should you fail and die on the way you will bu buried within King Tut's tomb forever.
