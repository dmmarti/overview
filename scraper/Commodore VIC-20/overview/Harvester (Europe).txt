Delta and Psi are two moons of the gas giant Meridien in the Altair System. 
 
Psi is inhabited by amorphous hydrosilicon lifeforms that cancommunicate on a telepatic level.  
 
On Delta, Boosterspice is farmed it is a narcotic with unpredictable side effects and outlawed on most other planets. It is however relatively harmless and essential part of the Deltan lifestyle
