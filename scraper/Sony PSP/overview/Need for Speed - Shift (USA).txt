Need for Speed Shift combines a fast-paced racing experience, competitive racing AI, dynamic crashes, across a wide range of technically challenging race tracks. Players are thrust into the loud, visceral, intense, athletic experience of racing a car on the edge of control. Need for Speed Shift features challenging driving model that allows you to feel every impact, every drift and every last bit of grip as you push yourself to the edge. Racing battles on city circuits, coastal highways and twisty mountain roads await in this portable racing experience.
