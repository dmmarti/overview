Bring up the knights of the round table. You are the master of knights with students of various backgrounds, some of them are elves, others are princesses, take them into the dungeons, solve the mysteries inside and unlock the beautiful event scenes.Everything is delicate in this RPG, including the enemies. The system is easy to learn but offers a variety of gameplay options, collect items and develop your characters, conquer the maps and discover individual character stories.This Sony PSP is an upgrade from the existing Xbox360 version, there are more illustrations, monsters and even new score, so come back to join the round table again.
