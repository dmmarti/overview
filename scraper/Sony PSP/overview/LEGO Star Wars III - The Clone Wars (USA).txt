Lego Star Wars III: The Clone Wars is a 2011 Lego game for the PlayStation 3, PlayStation Portable, Xbox 360, Wii, Nintendo DS, Microsoft Windows and Nintendo 3DS consoles, and based on The Clone Wars animated series, developed by Traveller's Tales and published by LucasArts and TT Games, released in March 2011[2] and is the latest game of the Lego Star Wars video game series. Lego Star Wars III features missions and characters from the Clone Wars television series, as well as favourite characters from the original Star Wars saga, in both single-player and multiplayer gameplay modes.[3][4][5] The Mac OS X version of the game has been released by Feral Interactive.[1]
