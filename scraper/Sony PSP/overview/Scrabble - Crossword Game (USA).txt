Scrabble offers a variety of ways to play the world's most popular and challenging word game. The game offers something for every crossword puzzle fan with three modes of play including: Classic Scrabble, which offers traditional play; Speed Scrabble, where players race the clock; and, the fast-paced Scrabble Slam card game where players try to be the first to discard all their SLAM cards by making four letter words. With six difficulty levels, Scrabble serves up a Training mode with six training exercises that enable new or rusty players to polish their skills before their first big showdown.
