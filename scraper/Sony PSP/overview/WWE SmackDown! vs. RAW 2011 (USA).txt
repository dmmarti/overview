WWE SmackDown vs. Raw 2011 (also shortened to WWE SvR 2011 or "SVR 11") is a professional wrestling video game developed by Yuke's and published by THQ for the PlayStation Portable (PSP). It is the seventh game in the WWE SmackDown vs. Raw series and the final game under the SmackDown vs. Raw name, and the final WWE game for the PSP.  It is the sequel to WWE SmackDown vs. Raw 2010. It was released on October 26, 2010 in North America, October 28 in Australia, October 29 in Europe, and February 3, 2011 in Japan for the PS3 and Xbox 360 versions. The game is based on the two brands of WWE, Raw and SmackDown.
