EA Sports brings the famed Tiger Woods Golf series to the PSP.  Experience the most detailed and feature filled handheld golf title to date.  All the courses and features of the console title are present plus all new features unique to the PSP.  Play on your favorite courses from both the 2004 and 2005 versions of Tiger Woods console and complete against the golfing greats of today and yesterday.  Add in WIFI, the brand new Bingo-Bango-Bongo gamemode and amazing graphics and you have a very enjoyable and exciting golf title for Sony's new PSP!
