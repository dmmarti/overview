The Legend of Heroes is a turn-based role-playing game set in the fantasy world of El Philden. The Legend of Heroes features hundreds of characters to interact with, including 14 playable ones, such as Avin, a hero searching for his sister in the aftermath of a great war. You can explore 3D environments as you progress through more than 50 hours of gameplay. The Legend of Heroes also expands upon other games in the genres with the inclusion of pets that can assist you in battle and collect items.
