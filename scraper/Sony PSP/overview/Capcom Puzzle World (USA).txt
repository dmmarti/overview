Capcom Puzzle World is a compilation of Capcom's retro arcade puzzle hits. There are some bonus features to unlock and play with. The games included are:  1. Block Block 2. Buster Bros. 3. Buster Buddies 4. Super Buster Bros. 5. Super Puzzle Fighter II Turbo (with bonus features based on two Japanese-only versions of the game)  Some games have multiplayer support, with both head-to-head and cooperative 2-player gameplay, and the ability to take screenshots. Block Block can be played with several aspect ratios including normal, stretch and the original vertical orientation.
