Bring to life an adventure of intrigue, deceit and revelation in Avatar: The Last Airbender, based on the popular Nickelodeon show. Play as Aang, Katara, Haru and Sokka as you grow your team into an unstoppable force and make discoveries in this thrilling blend of magic and martial arts. Use your fully customisable bending powers to master the four elements of Earth, Air, Water and Fire and explore the beautifully detailed environments of the Avatar nations.
