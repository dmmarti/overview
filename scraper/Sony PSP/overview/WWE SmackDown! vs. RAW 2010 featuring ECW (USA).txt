Get ready the get the smack laid down on you once again with the '010 version of WWE SmackDown vs. Raw 2010. Stepping back into the virtual ring, players will experience unparalleled creation tools, a robust Superstar roster, key franchise improvements and even a few surprises in WWE SmackDown vs. Raw 2010.    Players can embrace a directorial role to create and customize unique storylines with the WWE Story designer feature. Construct each night's show card with matches, unique match stipulations, interference events, and other story twists and turns. Also, the popular Road to Wrestlemania story-based mode returns with key updates and new storylines, including a first-ever Divas storyline and a Create-a-Superstar storyline.
