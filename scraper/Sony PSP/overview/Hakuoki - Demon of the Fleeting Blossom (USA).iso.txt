hakuoki: warriors of the shinsengumi is the first action game from the hit series hakuoki. As a member of a legendary group of warriors from bygone japan, battle against imperial forces, demons, and more in the first hack/slash game from this critically acclaimed series. Based on the visual novel, hakuoki: demon of the fleeting blossom, hakuoki: warriors of the shinsengumi brings together the great storytelling that defines this series and action-packed adventure for everyone to enjoy.
