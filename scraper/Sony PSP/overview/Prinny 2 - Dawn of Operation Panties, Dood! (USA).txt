Prinny 2: Dawn of Operation Panties, Dood!  is the sequel to the PSP game Prinny: Can I Really Be the Hero?. The game features the same gameplay as the original with several new abilities and features.    Etna is still in charge of the Netherworld while Laharl is working on his reincarnation. One night, Etna goes berserk due to the fact that someone broke into the Overlord's Castle and stole her panties. After causing mayhem, Etna sends 1000 Prinnies out to find the culprit and return her stolen underwear.
