The King of Fighters Collection: The Orochi Saga contains emulated versions of the first five games in the King of Fighters series:  The King of Fighters '94  The King of Fighters '95  The King of Fighters '96  The King of Fighters '97  The King of Fighters '98: Dream Match Never Ends  The package also includes a Challenge mode, where you can unlock over two hundred bonus goodies, like character art and music from the games.
