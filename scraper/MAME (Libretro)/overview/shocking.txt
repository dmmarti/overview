Shocking is a charming action adventure game wish some puzzle elements.  You are a werewolf seeking to return to your normal form and this quest will lead you to save the world.    Each round features a new objective to complete in order to clear the single screen level and continue on your journey.  There are 12 rounds in each stage and each stage ends with a boss battle.  Interspersed within the stages are various mini-game challenges where the player can earn extra lives.    The game is over once you have lost all of your lives.
