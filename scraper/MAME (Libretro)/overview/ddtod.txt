Dungeons &amp; Dragons: Tower of Doom is the first of two video games created by Capcom based on the Dungeons &amp; Dragons tabletop role-playing game. The game was also released on the Sega Saturn, packaged with its sequel, Dungeons &amp; Dragons: Shadow over Mystara, under the title Dungeons &amp; Dragons Collection, although the Saturn version limited the gameplay to only 2 players. The game is set in the Mystara campaign setting.
