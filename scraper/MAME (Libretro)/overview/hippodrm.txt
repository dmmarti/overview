The brave knight takes on a slew of medieval opponents. Money won during fights can be used to purchase fighter upgrades.    The game has 9 different selectable colosseums, each with a different opponent to face:  Pandemonium: Cawnus the Lamia Noble  Gehenna: Gran the Gargoyle  Tarterus: Norfolk the Lizard Man  Gadsheim: Sharon the Armor Dragon  Archeron: Daldnoa the Scorpion Man  Elysium: Solomon the Wizard  Twin Paradises: Charry Steve The Assassins  Abyss: Pon the Giant
