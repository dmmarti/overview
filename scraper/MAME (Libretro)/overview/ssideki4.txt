Super Sidekicks 4, also known as The Ultimate 11: SNK Football Championship, is a sports retro arcade football (soccer) game created for the Neo Geo arcade system, and is the fourth of the popular Super Sidekicks series. The game features 80 national teams divided into 8 regions of the globe. Build power the more you hold the ball. Maintain your dribble and guard off against enemy steals. Amass enough foot power and kick the ball to the goal!
