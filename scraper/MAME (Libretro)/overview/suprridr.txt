Become the stunt bike racer you've always dreamed of as you speed along the colourful courses at break neck speeds to perform the most amazing tricks the world has ever seen! In the brilliant Super Rider you race around the place, overtaking other speedy dudes, pulling crazy tricks and even riding upside down as you ride the loop the loops! Jump gaps, reach max speeds, follow the track where ever it takes you to beat the game and score big!
