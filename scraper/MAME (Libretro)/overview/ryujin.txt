The title of this game translates from Japanese as 'Dragon God'. The player can choose between 4 different ships, each equiped with 4 basic weapon (Vulcan, Laser, Homing Missile and Option Laser) and a unique special attack:  Usable fighter &amp; special attack:  Sword Breaker (Blue) - Fireball Bomb  Vaulge (Green) - Lightning Volt  Shamseel (Red) - Flash Bomb  Phalcyon (Purple) - Wave Attack
