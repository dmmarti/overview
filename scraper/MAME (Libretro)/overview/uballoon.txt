On a seemingly peaceful night in the Robinson household there are strange things afoot in the baby's room... the toys are launching a revolution and are creating a crazy mess! You must help the cool robot-controlling infant and save him from the evil blue monster. Take the part of a cool blue penguin and navigate through the increasingly tricky levels to solve the puzzles and save the day! Jump between platforms and solve the puzzles to stop the evil plans of the blue monster!    A "Bubble Bobble" clone.
