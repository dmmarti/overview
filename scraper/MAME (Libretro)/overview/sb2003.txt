An advanced version of a classic type of game. Moni &amp; Chris are on a quest to rid there island of viscous invading monsters. Players blow bubble to entrap the monsters and then jump on them to make them pop. Luckily Moni &amp; Chris have many items to help them dispose of these dangerous foes. Like exterminate scrolls which called forth the force of the elements and nature or the super transform crystal which powers up our heroes so they don't have to encase the enemies in bubbles first they can just run into they to destroy them. Players can in power up the attack button to blow a triple bubble instead of a single. The main objective of the players is to clear all the enemies for each area of the island. The game consists of 4 chapters with 60 stages.
