A vertical scrolling jet shoot them up, featuring both single &amp; co-operative gameplay as well as a myriad of power ups. The players get to choose from 5 different jets each with it's own unique attributes &amp; weapons. By collecting varies power ups during the missions the player's jet with be transformed into a stronger, more powerful version.nnThe jet selection screen music was ripped from the first stage music of the early SNK Neo-Geo MVS shoot-em-up game "Andro Dunos".
