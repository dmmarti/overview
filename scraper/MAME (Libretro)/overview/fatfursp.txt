Fatal Fury Special is an improved version of SNK's 2D one-on-one fighter Fatal Fury 2. It's a conversion of the 1993 arcade game of the same name. The objective is still to win the tournament by defeating all other fighters including end boss Wolfgang Krauser. Fatal Fury Special features a fairly large selection of fighters. A new Count Down mode (defeat as many opponents as possible in 3 minutes).Extra color schemes for every character.New &amp; improved background graphics.Dolby Surround Sound support.
