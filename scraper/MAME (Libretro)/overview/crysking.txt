A nice beat'em up featuring transparencies, rowscroll effects and eight levels...  Stage 1: The Pull Off Evil - Boss: Great Destructer  Stage 2: Across The Currents - Boss: Serpent  Stage 3: The Cave Of Darkness - Boss: Master Of Underground  Stage 4: The Congress Of Cystals - Boss: Harpies  Stage 5: Into The Darkness - Boss: 2 Minotaurs  Stage 6: Estorea - Boss: Sinister Dragon  Stage 7: Nero Filone - Boss: Black Knight  Stage 8: Crystal Of The King - Boss: Night Spirit, Dark Spirit
