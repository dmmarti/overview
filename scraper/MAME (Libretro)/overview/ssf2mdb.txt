Super Street Fighter II: The New Challengers is the fourth game in the Street Fighter II sub-series of Street Fighter games, following Street Fighter II: Hyper Fighting. In addition to refining and balancing the existing character roster from the previous installments, Super Street Fighter II also introduced four new characters.    This game is also known under the name of Super Street Fighter II: The Tournament Battle when 4 Cabinets are connected to play in tournament mode.
