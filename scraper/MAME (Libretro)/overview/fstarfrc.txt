A sequel to Tehkan's 1984 shoot-em-up classic, "Star Force", featuring improved graphics and sound and a new weapons power-up system. The player's ship is equipped with three smart bombs and the fire button can be held down for rapid fire. At regular intervals, a red ship will appear which will release a power-up capsule; the three power-ups available are as follows:  A-Pulsator:   Fire: Thunder + Homing   Bomb: S-Bomb  B-Pulsator:   Fire: Wide Shot + Missile   Bomb: M-Bomb  C-Pulsator:   Fire: Spraygun + Synch-Fire   Bomb: T-Bomb
