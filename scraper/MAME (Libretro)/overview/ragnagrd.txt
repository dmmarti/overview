This is a fighting game originaly designed in 3D , and 2D sprites transposed (known as Computer Graphics, or CG), like Donkey Kong Country or Pulstar. Story: It is a world occupied by gods, the world in which reign the god of the Arc of Orion. He decided to offer his succession in a tournament at eight gods. The winner will have access to the throne of the god of the Arc of Orion. A struggle between the gods is about to begin.
