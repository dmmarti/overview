The totally awesome and powerful Sonic Blastman is back and this time he's looking for some more crime fighting action from crazy evil beings trying to wreak havoc on planet Earth! In the badass Sonic Blast Man II you hit the streets with a vengeance and start knocking heads to fight through all the bad guys and save the planet! Brawl and blast your way through terrorists, aliens, strange beasts and much more as you fight for the survival of humanity!
