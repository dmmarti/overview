A futuristic racing game simulator.    The player takes the wheel of a high tech yellow car, through psicodelic futuristics courses, where they are tested on number of different areas. The game featuring nice techno music, and the gameplay is based on driving symbols that appear on the top right of the screen. These symbols correspond to different driving test such as Fast Thinking, Power Control, Smooth Driving, Drift King and more. A total of 15 tests are included in the game. Tests are grouped into three's, players either pass or fail the test and then continue onto the next one. You must accelerate fast and maintain control in tricky curves and jump ramps while dodging objects to win the race.
