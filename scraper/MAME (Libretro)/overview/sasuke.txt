A great vertical shoot'em up game.     You, controls a redheaded ninja warrior named Sasuke, set in feudal Japan in the beautiful city of kioto must protect your master the Shogun, by defeating all the villains ninja Commander.     The player Sasuke is always at the bottom of the screen can only move left or right and shoot tiny shuriken, he faces off against multiple ninjas stealthily jumping from trees and several powerfull bosses each equipped with their own magics capabilities such as Henshin, Bunshin, Kaen and Hiryuken you must eliminate all the enemies and dodge his falling corpses, to complete the stage.
