'Crazy War' is a video game revolving around an epic battle between the Coalition of Planets, and the evil Devilion Empire.    In the year 544 the Devilion Empire has invaded the calm galaxy to expand its territory. The Coalition of Planets has selected seven special personnel to fight the Devilion Empire, and the player takes on the role of one of these seven, embarking on a mission to destroy the Devilion Empire's Evil Stone. The mission is completed over 13 levels.    Crazy War is a video game for 1 or 2 players. In 1-player mode, become one of the 7 special personnel and perform your role to destroy the Evil Stone. In 2-player mode, both players fight against each other.
