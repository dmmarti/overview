Play as 8 world class ATP / WTA tour players (including Mark Philippoussis, Richard Krajicek, Conchita Martinez & Jana Novotna) as well as 4 fictional players through a range of tournaments on grass, clay, hard court and rebound ace surfaces.

Lob, volley, smash and serve past computer controlled players in singles or doubles, 4 player multiplayer doubles, and 8 player multiplayer singles and doubles tournaments.
