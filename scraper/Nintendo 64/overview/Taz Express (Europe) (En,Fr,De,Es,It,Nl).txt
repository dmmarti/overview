Tazmania inspired Taz the Tasmanian Devil, is a well known Warner Brothers character which this game expands on. This 3D puzzle game see's Taz delivering crates across 25 levels and 5 game worlds including Mars, the Bushlands, Monument Valley and the Wild West. Spin, run, jump onto skyscrapers, jump with various springy devices, swim, and fly with a jetpack through levels to complete tasks and puzzles in the bright coloured cartoon environments. Complete the game four times to unlock all the secrets and discover what is in the crate Taz is delivering.
