The final QC title on N64 follows in the similar footsteps of the previous three titles on the console, with a full on NFL football simulation with all the teams, players and stats.

Modes include Exhibition, Season, Practice & Pro Bowl, with the option to play past NFL Super Bowl games our create your own custom simulation.

You can also create your own team and players, a full play book, and sign or trade players pre-season. Up to 4 players are supported.
