QC returns for another season. For the third time around, improvements over the previous titles include far more player detail and animations during plays (thanks to 1200 motion captured moves) and more moves and strategies.

In terms of gameplay, the usual assortment of options are included, from full team management (trades, drafting, etc.) to create a player and create a team modes. Play with 4 people in Tournament, Season, Playoffs, Pro Bowl and Exhibition games, along with the chance to re-live past Super Bowls.
