Acclaim and Iguana come together for a full-on NFL football title. QC98 features full season, exhibition and playoff modes right through the the Super Bowl, with every team, player and stat from the 1998 season.

You can also play historic Super Bowl games from past years, where you play as a specific team at a point in one of the many games, and attempt to re-create history. And if that isn't enough, you can also read full profiles on every player in the league.
