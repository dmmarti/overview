Monster Truck Madness 64 is a monster truck racing game for the Nintendo 64. The game features 20 trucks to ride in and 10 tracks to race on.

Single player game modes the game offers are exhibition and circuit race. During the races you have to drive to check points to set your time. You can pick up a variety of power-ups such as invisibility, homing missiles, oil slicks, shrink bombs, hover modes, nitro, super jumps and shields.

In multi-player the game offers various game modes such as hockey, soccer, police chase, summit rumble (king of the hill) and tag.

You can play the tracks during various types of weather and different times of the day.
