Adventure, Mini-games, and Multiplayer Action await you! Bomberman Land Touch 2 is the follow up sequel to IGN's Best Online Multiplayer 2006 Game of the Year on Nintendo DS. Embark on a fun-filled, theme park adventure as you discover a huge land to explore, a gigantic multiplayer pack, and a host of addicting mini-games that can be played by yourself or with up to 4 people
