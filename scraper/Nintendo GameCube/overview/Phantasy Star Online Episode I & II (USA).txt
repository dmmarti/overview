The epic Phantasy Star Online gets revamped for the Nintendo GameCube. Both the Sega Dreamcast titles, Phantasy Star Online and Version 2, are included plus an exclusive new adventure, dungeons, enemies, new rare items and more.

Phantasy Star Online allows up to 4 players to co-operate online or on the one GameCube console in an adventure spanning countless fantasy battles in highly detailed worlds. Four players can also play a range of different mini-game types including Battle Mode and Challenge Mode.
