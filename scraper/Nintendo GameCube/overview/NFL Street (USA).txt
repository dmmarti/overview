NFL STREET is an arcade style football game that is based on pick up football. There are no helmets or stadiums in NFL STREET. NFL STREET is 7 on 7 with players playing both offense and defense.  The games are played in parks, on the street and in the dirt lots.

In NFL STREET you should strive not just to win but to look good doing it.  You achieve this by performing style moves during a play.  These might be a taunting run, a spectacular spin, or a crazy pass.

NFL Street has Quick Game, Pickup Game, NFL Challenge and Online game modes.
