Sonic & the gang, are back in the latest game, SONIC HEROES. Coming out on the Gamecube & for the first
time on Playstation 2,  Xbox And PC! Sonic Heroes has a twist to the
gameplay, you choose a team of 3 characters. With different strengths. 
There are 4 teams:
TEAM SONIC ~ Sonic, Tails & Knuckles
TEAM DARK ~ Shadow, Rouge & E-123 Omega
TEAM CHAOTIX ~ Espio, Vector, & Charmy Bee
TEAM ROSE ~  Amy, Big, & Cream with her Chao sidekick Cheese.
There are 4 modes, Story mode, Challenge mode,
the Tutorial modes & the new & improved battle mode for
multiplayer fun,
and there are plenty of secrets to unlock.
