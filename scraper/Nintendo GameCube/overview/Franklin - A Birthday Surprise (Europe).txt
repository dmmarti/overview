Discover the wonderful world of Franklin in 10 great platform levels! 
Unlock the 10 arcade-type mini-games and replay them whenever you want in single or multi-player option 
The game has two exciting game modes: Adventure mode and Battle mode 
Play & interact with the beloved characters from Franklin's universe: Franklin the Turtle, his good friends Bear and Beaver and Franklin's mother. 
Set in a 3D environment, whilst remaining true to the original look & personality of theFranklin the Turtle TV series
