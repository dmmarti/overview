Cabela's Outdoor Adventures provides you with the hunting and fishing expedition of a lifetime with 22 outdoor adventures across North America and beyond. Fly, float, and trek from Utah to Mexico to New Zealand in search of trophy bucks or stop off and angle for the local species of fish. While on your adventure you'll also have the opportunity to hunt both big game and small game, compete in a shooting contest, and embark on varied missions. Your Cabela's Guide introduces each true-to-life outdoor adventure where stealth strategy and technique are of the utmost importance.
