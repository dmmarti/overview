The devious Yosemite Sam has captured Taz and locked him up as a circus animal and is planning to turn Taz's beloved homeland into a amusement park.

But then Taz successfully escapes from the clutches of Sam and once again is going on the rampage to stop Sam's plans for his homeland.

Blast your way through 15 different levels, each of which can be destroyed using spin, bounce and burp attacks.
You can get foods such as mage hot chili and soda to aid your mission of vengeance against Sam.
