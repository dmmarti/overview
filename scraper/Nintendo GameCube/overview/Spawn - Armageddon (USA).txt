Spawn, the popular comic book character created by Todd McFarlane appears on the next generation consoles in this 3-D action/adventure game from Namco Hometek Inc.  Developed by Point of View, Spawn: Armageddon features a compelling storyline contributed by Todd McFarlane.  Spawn struggles between the forces of Heaven and Hell in order to save the world.  Spawn is equipped with his Hell-sent living symbiotic suit which includes the Chains.  A multitude of weapons and Hell Powers are also available.  Agony, the Axe that Spawn uses in Soul Calibur II is also available as a melee attack weapon.
