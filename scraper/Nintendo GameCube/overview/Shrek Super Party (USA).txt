Join Shrek and the entire cast from the hit movie for the a party game that will determine the best player in the land.

The aim of the game is simple. Move along the game board and collect as much bug juice as you can. Each square holds either the chance to steal some from another player, or lose some yourself, while game squares have you playing off for juice in a range of mini-games.

30 mini games await either you or 4 people, both all-on-all or two-on-two style. Games include puzzle variants, racing and more.
