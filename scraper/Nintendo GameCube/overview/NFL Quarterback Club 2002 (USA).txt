This is the eighth installment of the highly acclaimed Quarterback Club series. This edition features all of the teams and players of the current NFL ranks, and allows you to unlock legendary passers like John Elway, Dan Marino, and Steve Young. Kevin Harlan and Bill Maas provide commentary.

The game features a total of four primary modes of play: season, exhibition, playoffs, and pro bowl.

Also featured in QB Club 2002 is the quarterback challenge mode, which allows you to take your favorite NFL quarterback through a competition focusing on four different skill sets: speed and mobility, accuracy throw, read and recognition, and long distance throw.
