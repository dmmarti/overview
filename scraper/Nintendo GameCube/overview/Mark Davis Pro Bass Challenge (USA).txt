Mark Davis Pro Bass Challenge is a grand slam of a bass game! Compete in various tournaments as you work your way to the top and compete against the fishing master himself, Mark Davis! Strive to improve your ranking as you unlock all the latest tackle equipment. 
Features: 
Enhanced bass AI, so not only are the black bass faithfully reproduced on the screen, they also act just like real black bass! 
Official Mark Davis insightful fishing tips. 
Three modes of play: Tournament, Arcade, and Practice mode. 
Tons of tackle. 
Amateur, semi-pro, and professional tournaments. 
Enriched environments and seasonal weather patterns.
