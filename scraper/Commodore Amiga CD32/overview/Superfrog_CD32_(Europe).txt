Superfrog is a one of these rare traditional jump & run games. You’re – surprise – a frog. Your enemies are all animals – like bees, hedgehogs or snails. You have no weapons. The only way to kill the enemies is to jump on them. Every stage has a timelimit. In this limit you have to collect a pretended number of coins. Each of the 6 worlds has 4 stages. Every stage has a password. After all you can type your name into a hi score table.
