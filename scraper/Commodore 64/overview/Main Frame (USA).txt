The worlds greatest computer, TriComplex III, has decided that people are a bug in his program. In order to fix this problem it plans to wipe out the human race. You are humanity's only hope for survival. With the help of the orbiting space station and a power suit you platform your way though buildings, mountains, caverns, and high tech bases to defeat TriComplex. Main Frame is an arcade style adventure/platform game where the player will need to battle droids and secret weapons in order to shutdown TriComplexx III.
