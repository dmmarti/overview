You are walking along when you find a magic wand and after waving it about you have appeared in a strange land. As you explore this land you discover that your aim is to find a certain amount of valuable treasure. You will have to solve puzzles and interact with various fantasy creatures and people. Faerie is a text adventure where the text on the screen describes your location and objects that can be seen. You have to type in commands with the keyboard to interact with your locations, objects and other creatures.
