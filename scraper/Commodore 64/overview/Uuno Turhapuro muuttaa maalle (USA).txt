This game is based on the movie of the same name. Uuno Turhapuro Muuttaa Maalle is a game about poor looking guy from Finland, a kind of loser, who lives in a city and one day finds a letter in the refrigerator. The letter is from his wife Elizabeth, who says that she has moved out and her lawyers will contact him. Uuno doesn't like living in an empty house so he decides to move to the countryside. There he gets to do different tasks like ploughing the fields, water skiing and run away from an angry neighbour. During gameplay, screen scrolls from left to right. You control character Uuno with keyboard or joystick. When ploughing a field, watch out for rocks and other obstacles. You will also earn money when you do this. When Uuno water skies he encounters water animals like ducks and birds.
