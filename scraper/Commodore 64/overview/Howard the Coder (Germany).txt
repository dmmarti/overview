In Howard the Cover the player takes the role of Howard who wants to impress his girlfriend. To do so, he collects fragments of his code so he can finish his program. This game is a scrolling platformer in which the player has walk and jump through hazardous obstacles, e.g. moving spikes or bats. The goal is to collect said code fragments and computer parts. This is the main portion of the game, but when it is finished the player also can play Howard's game: a logic puzzle.
