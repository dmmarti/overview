On the Venus 4 stellar base a revolt broke out and the Venusians led by Lady K., a bitter enemy of the Terrans, came to power.nnGo on a mission on board your ship. But be careful, as soon as you enter the orbit of Venus 4 you will have to disengage from your ship and proceed to free flight. At first your ship will protect you from attack, but you can then rely solely on your skills.
