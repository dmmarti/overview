Ring Of Power is a single player text adventure with graphics. In a distant land people live happy and contented lives under the rule of the Colourful King. Then, one day, the king lost his mind and could not find it. Thus the crown jewels were lost for only the king knew where they, and the 'Ring of Power' were hidden. Thus begins the adventure. The player must search the land for items of treasure items. When all key items have been found the player can locate the 'Ring of Power' and thus become the new king and win the game. The game features a text parser which accepts two word commands as well as the standard abbreviations, 'N for Go North' etc.
