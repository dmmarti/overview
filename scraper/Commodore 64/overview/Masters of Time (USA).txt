All history on Hearth has been lost!nnYou are the pilot of a time machine. Faster than the speed of light you travel back through a time-stream. Your assignment: reach and record past events on Earth. Arriving at different time stratas, you will find the time holograms, but watch out for the timeguards and timebombs who are trying to destroy your mission. When you discover and capture a timehologram, you will automatically receive digitilized information about the event.nnWhen your energy threatens to run out, you must go into intertimelevel. Crisscrossing through the Universe you arrive at the present. To become a Master of Time you must program your computer data base with the information you discovered in the timestream.nnMasters of Time is in full color 3-dimensional graphics and includes an EDITOR program allowing the players to create their own illustrated history!
