Three-on-three basketball game featuring Patrick Ewing, Joe Dumars and Dominique Wilkins. Play as the 'Dream Team' or against the 'Dream Team'. The game features asphalt and hardwood courts in a few different locations. The game has 15 set plays that the players can execute, and a playmaker utility that allows you to come up with your own plays. The game also has a player-management utility that allows you to edit player attributes.
