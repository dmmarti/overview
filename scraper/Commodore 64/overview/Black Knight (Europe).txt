The evil Black Knight has ripped through the valley and destroyed buildings and killed and captured the people. You are the hero who will find and kill the Knight and bring order back to the valley. You must search the surrounding areas to get the objects and weapons needed before entering a castle, kill the guards before defeating The Black Knight. Black Knight Adventure is a a text adventure that has text on screen that describes your location. You type in commands with the keyboard to interact with your surroundings.
