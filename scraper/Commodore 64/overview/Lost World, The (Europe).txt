You are Edward Malone, a reporter for the Daily Gazette. Dispatches you have been getting from your friend Maple White have stopped coming. Maple had been describing a lost world he had discovered. Thinking the worst you set out for this world. Arriving at the base camp below plateau cliffs you discover that Maple has died. You want to find out how he died and also explore this strange land. Just how to get to the top of the cliffs The Lost World is a text adventure with no graphics. On the screen you can read about your surroundings as well as type in commands to discover progress on your adventure.
