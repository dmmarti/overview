Droid is a single board shooter. The game takes place on the planet Xeviou, taken over by bandits. As the land is full of natural resources the Highest Intergalactic Council decides to send scientifically advanced robot to neutralize the central computer. Unfortunately, robot was not equipped with defense mechanisms therefore protector is needed to complete the task. Droid moves constantly according to the programmed sequence on each board. The player moves viewfinder and must kill all bandits appearing in various places before they dispose the robot. After passing each board Droid receives the full amount of energy. The game is divided into five stages, each containing five levels.
