U.S.S. John Young is a 2D naval simulation with many action components. In 1995, the political situation in the Persian Gulf is running out of control. There are confrontations between Iranian, Iraqi and US military units. As the Captain of the US Destroyer U.S.S. John Young, it's your mission to secure this area. Your operations during this conflict : Subhunter missions Seek and Destroy missions Convoy protections War patrols
