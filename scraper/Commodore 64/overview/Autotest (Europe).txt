Autotest Simulator is top view driving game where the screen scrolls in various directions when you move the car. The aim of the game is to drive round various courses as fast as you can within a qualifying time to earn money and points for the National Championship Table. Before you drive you can select a car from a choice of four with each car handling different. The course is made up of cones and you must follow the course to various boxes which you either have to drive forwards in or reverse in, with bad driving giving your penalty points. As you drive you also have the ability to do a handbrake turn forwards and backwards to change direction quickly. Once you cross the finishing line your time and penalty points are totaled and if your outside the qualifying time then it is game over. Get within the time and you receive points and money if you finish in the top two against three other drivers. You then are taken to a garage to upgrade various parts of your car before you then go and attempt the next course. Up to four players can play with with player taking it in turns once a player finishes a course.
