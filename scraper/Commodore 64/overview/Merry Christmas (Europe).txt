Merry Christmas from Melbourne House is a short graphical text adventure for kids. It's Christmas Eve and there is trouble at the North Pole. Can you help Santa to finish getting ready and deliver the presents on time Players will have to solve a few simple puzzles while exploring the North Pole to help Santa on his way. The game was released for 95p as mail-away for readers of Commodore User magazine in December 1984. The game includes ads for other Melbourne House software products available for sale for Christmas 1984.
