You have discovered a map of Skull Island in your Great Grandfather's sea chest containing cryptic signs and a treasure chest. You have decided to find the treasure, so standing on beach on the island you set out to decipher the map and find hopefully the treasure hidden on the island. The game is a graphic text adventure where the screen has a picture for each location you visit and text describing what you see. You type in commands with the keyboard to interact with your surroundings.
