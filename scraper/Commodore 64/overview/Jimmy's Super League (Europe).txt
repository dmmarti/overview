Here's your chance to manage a football team and play against the best teams in Europe in a league format. After selecting your team you are now sitting in your office in a 1st person perspective. Various parts of the office allow you to pick your team, buy and sell players, scout players, get a loan and look at your league position. The week number and how much money you have are shown. Once you're happy with your selections you can then move onto the next game. At the top of the screen a side view of one half of the pitch is shown. Below the pitch is a description of the action. When a goal mouth action appears you will see a player and a goalkeeper. The player shoots and a save or goal is shown. The two teams, score and game time are displayed at the bottom of the screen
