You are a private investigator and after receiving a phone call from Mrs Gloria F. Bannister, you agree to search for her necklace which has been stolen before her husband finds out it's missing. You must move around a city looking for clues, questioning various people as well as receive other jobs and face various dangers. The Jade Necklace is a a text adventure that has text on screen that describes your location. You type in commands with the keyboard to interact with your surroundings.
