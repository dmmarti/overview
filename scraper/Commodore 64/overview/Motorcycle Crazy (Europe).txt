On your motorcycle, you have entered the Motor Cycle Rally where you start and finish at Ariel Castle searching the surrounding area for the Award. On your ride you must find various objects to use in the correct places as well as find equipment to help you in your search. Motorcycle Crazy is a graphic text adventure where the screen has text describing each location you visit and a picture of what you can see. You type in commands with the keyboard to interact with your surroundings.
