The Master Control Unit which controls all machines and robots that the humans rely on has decided that humans are no longer needed in the world and has reprogrammed everything to kill the humans. You are a survivor of the massacre and your mission is to shut down the MCU but it needs a code and your friend who programmed it gave parts of the code to all his friends but all are dead. Starting in a guest room you must search your hometown for the pieces of code avoiding any robot guards. Skegpool Rock: Operation Annihilation is a graphics text adventure where each screen has text describing your location with some screens have a picture showing what you can see. You type in commands to control your hero and interact with your locations.
