Oh You want to leave already I'm afraid that's simply not possible... not until you have taken the complete tour. The Castles of Dr. Creep is a platform game with a heavy puzzle element in the game. The game takes place in twelve medieval castles owned by the eponymous Dr. Creep, and your task is to escape from each castle. One or two-player game is possible, allowing collaborative gaming for solving the puzzles.
