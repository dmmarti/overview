The world is on the verge of World War III when M.A.S.T.A., a giant defense computer belonging to NORAD, has developed intelligence and is playing war. You play the role of its creator, Dr. Norton, as you attempt to get into the command centre of NORAD, housed inside Cheyenne Mountain, find a security disk and the access code. Can you save the World from being destroyed Thermo Nuclear War Games is a graphic text adventure where the screen shows a picture of what you can see, while text describes your location. You type in commands with the keyboard to interact with your surroundings.
