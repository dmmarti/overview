Final Whistle is a football management game where you select an English team and try to get them to win the league and the FA Cup and League Cup. As well as selecting your team you can decide your starting division from Division 4 to Division 1 before being shown your next match to be played. You are presented with various options to help you as well as looking at the stats of your team and your opponents and you can scout them or any other team in any of the divisions. You can select your starting 11 from a squad of players and change various tactics like the formation. There is also a transfer market where you can buy or sell players. Once you are happy with your selections you then watch your match in progress which is shown by text with the major incidents of the game also mentioned. After the game you are shown your finances before the next match is shown and you select options once again. If your bank account is overdrawn for more than three weeks or you finish bottom of Division 4 then you will be sacked. There are three skill levels to choose and they are easy, normal and hard.
