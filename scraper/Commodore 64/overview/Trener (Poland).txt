Trener is a soccer management game. The game allows you to choose one of the following options: Polish League, League of Nations, Europa League. In the first case the player can choose one of the eighteen teams from Extraklasa Polish League. In the second and third case the player has no choice - directs Poland and Legia Warsaw in leagues consisting of eighteen teams. The game offers the opportunity to carry out training, setting tactics, buying and selling players, determine the premium. During the match there is no animation. The match takes place in the form of text messages. Team manager may make changes in the composition, determine the tactics and level of aggression. After the match the game shows the results of the round and the current table. At the end of the season the club receives bonuses determined by the place in the table. The game has no sound.
