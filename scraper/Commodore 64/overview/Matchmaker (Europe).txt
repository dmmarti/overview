After all your training you now face your final test to receive the title of Matchmaker and it involves getting a Prince and Princess to the church to get married. Other tasks are to make sure they are dressed for the occasion and to get a correctly dressed page boy as well. You have to move around your village and surrounding areas entering buildings and shops to interact with people who will give tasks to receive the items you need. Match Maker is a text adventure where you read text on the screen which describes each location and you type in commands to control your hero and interact with various locations.
