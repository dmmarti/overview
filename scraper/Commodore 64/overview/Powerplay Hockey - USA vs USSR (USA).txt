Powerplay Hockey is a ice hockey game concentrating on the game between the USA and the USSR. Before starting the game you are to choose the number of players (on-on-one or five-on-five) and length of match (two, tvelwe or twenty minutes per period). When in the game try wrist shots, slap shots, faceoff, drop passing, and body checking. Roughing and crosscheking will give you a penalty.
