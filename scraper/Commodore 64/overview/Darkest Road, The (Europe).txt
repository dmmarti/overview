The Pinnacle lies far to the North and is home to the Black Wanderer who is using his evil magic to cast a threatening shadow over your land. A stranger has entered your village with knowledge on how to defeat the Black Wanderer. A gift from your Father is the key, Mystical Magic. After spending many hours learning the use of the magic with the stranger you set off on your adventure to defeat the Black Wanderer. The game is a text only adventure with no graphics. As well as a description of your surroundings, you enter commands to solve this adventure.
