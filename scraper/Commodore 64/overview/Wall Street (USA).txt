Become a Wall Street Trader with this managerial-simulation for up to 4 players. At the start of the game stands an test of your knowledge about sharetrading: The more questions you can answer correct, the more money you receive for the start of the game. Buy and sell shares and fonds to maximize your stock. Ingame you receive news from the market on which you must react. The game includes a program to manage your own depots without playing the game itself.
