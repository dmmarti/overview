Wimbledon'64 is a tennis game for two players viewed from one end of the court at an elevated angle. Before the game starts there are various options that can be selected and they are, Difficulty level (0-3), how many sets (1-3) and the speed of the ball (Slow or fast). The game follows the rules of tennis and the player has the ability to use backhand, forehand, lob and smash. The winner is the player to reach the number of sets chosen before the game.
