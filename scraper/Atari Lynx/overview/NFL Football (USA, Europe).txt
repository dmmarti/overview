NFL Football is an officially licensed NFL Americal football game. The game is played by holding the Lynx vertically and sports zooming and scaling graphical features, with an overhead, top-down view. Games last for four 15-minute quarters and it contains circa 25 offensive plays. Passing is done by holding down the B button and then moving a cross-hair to an open receiver, and then releasing the button to throw. Strategies can be changed in real-time. Two players can compete head-to-head with the ComLynx cable.
