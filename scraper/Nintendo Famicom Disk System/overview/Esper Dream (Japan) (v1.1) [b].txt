This is a story that occurred in the distant village from where you live. 
This village was filled with a beautiful, quiet, and mysterious aroma. 
 
However, the villagers suffered from an unexpected tragedy. 
The Evil clan called Narcisse tribe, began searching for the magic hidden in our village. 
 
Even worse, they kidnapped the daughter of our mayor, Alice. 
 
Please, go forth and rescue Alice, and bring peace to our land!
