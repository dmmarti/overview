Wai Wai World 2: SOS!! Parsley Jō (ワイワイワールド2 SOS!!パセリ城, Wai Wai Wārudo Tsū Esuōesu!! Paseri Jō, lit. "Wai Wai World 2: SOS!! Parsley Castle) is a 1991 Famicom platform game released only in Japan by Konami. It is a sequel to Konami Wai Wai World, and stars various Konami characters. It was also re-released for Wii U Virtual Console on September 2, 2015 in Japan.
