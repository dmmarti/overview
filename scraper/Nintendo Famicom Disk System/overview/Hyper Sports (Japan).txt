Hyper Sports, known in Japan as Hyper Olympic '84 (ハイパーオリンピック'84) is an arcade game published in 1984 by Konami. It is the sequel to 1983's Track &amp; Field and features seven all-new Olympic events. Like its predecessor, Hyper Sports has two run buttons and one action button per player. The Japanese release of the game sported an official license for the 1984 Summer Olympics.
