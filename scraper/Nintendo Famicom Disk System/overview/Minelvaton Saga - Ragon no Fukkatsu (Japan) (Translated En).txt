Minelvaton Saga: Ragon no Fukkatsu (ミネルバトンサーガ ラゴンの復活, Minelvaton Saga: Ragon's Revival) is a Role Playing Game published by Taito Corporation for the Family Computer. It is the first game in the Minelvaton series, being followed by Silva Saga and ending with Silva Saga II: The Legend of Light and Darkness.
