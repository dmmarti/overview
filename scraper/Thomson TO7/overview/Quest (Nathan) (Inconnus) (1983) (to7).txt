When playing Quest, you assume the character of a brave warrior, setting out to overcome the mighty forces of the evil Moorlock, master of the dark castle in the southern reaches of the realm.

When the question "What now sire?" appears, you have a choice of entering a direction of "NORTH", "SOUTH", "EAST", or "WEST", a "USE" instruction or an "INVENTORY" instruction. You only need to enter the first letter of the instruction to proceed.
