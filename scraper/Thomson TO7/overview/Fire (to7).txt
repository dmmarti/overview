Ungh was supposed to watch over his tribe’s fireplace during the night. But being a slightly clumsy Neanderthal, Ungh fell asleep and let the fire go out. Now it is Ungh’s task to acquire a new fire for his tribe. He is sent on a long and exciting journey which will take him through a unique Stone Age world, full of yet unseen, quirky creatures and gripping puzzles. On his journey, Ungh will ride on dinosaurs and might even find his true love.
