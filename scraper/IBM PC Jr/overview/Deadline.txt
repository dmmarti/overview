In DEADLINE, you're the Division. You are responsible for the lives of the hostages, your team and yourself. Your squard consists highly trained firearms officers that are on standby 24 hours a day. Strategic planning, careful weapons selection, and precise execution are necessary for success. With over 17 complex levels, real-time interaction, and full motion video briefings and debriefings, DEADLINE will put you up close and personal with the terrorists.
