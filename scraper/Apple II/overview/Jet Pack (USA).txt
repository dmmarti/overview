A software bundle of Novalogic's F-16 Multirole Fighter, MiG-29 Fulcrum, and an updated F-22 Raptor.  The patched F-22 allows it to fly on Novalogic's free Integrated Battle Space servers with up to 128 other pilots with any of those three games.    In total across the three games, this package features over 130 missions spread across the entire world, in campaigns and quick missions.
