Battle Dodge Ball II is the second Battle Dodge Ball in the Compati Hero Series and the fifth game from that series for the Super Famicom overall. It is the sequel to Banpresto's earlier Battle Dodge Ball.The game features characters from Kamen Rider, Ultraman and Gundam. Each team is comprised of heroes or villains from each of the three universes, and have their own array of special moves that can be used to damage the opposing team. The goal is to throw a dodgeball back and forth to reduce the HP of opposing team members. Once a whole team has been eliminated from play, the other side wins.Battle Dodge Ball II was the last game in the Dodge Ball sub-series until 2012's Battle Dodge Ball III for the PSP.
