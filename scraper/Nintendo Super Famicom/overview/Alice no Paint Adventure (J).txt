In Alice no Paint Adventure, there are three game play modes. In Story Mode, there are passwords for the levels, and an abridged version of the movie Alice in Wonderland can be viewed. To understand the game, players have to fluent in both English and Japanese. There are also three arcade games: Painting the Roses Red, Whack a Character, and a memory game. 
 
In Paint Mode, players have a full palette of colors and the ability to mix them. There is also an eraser, a spray tool, and a 256-color spectrum board to paint on.
