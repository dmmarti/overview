Classic Road II, sometimes Classic Road II: Real Kouma Simulation, is a horseracing simulation game from Opera House and Victor Entertainment. The player owns their own stables and can raise and race their own racehorses. They can also choose to bet on other races for additional funds. Players split their time between their stables and the betting track at their discretion.Classic Road II is the sequel to the 1993 Super Famicom game Classic Road. It did not see another sequel.
