Brightis is a 3d action adventure game that takes place in a fantasy world where the people have discovered an ancient ruin and dug out a magical stone. They mistakenly unseal the gate of evil and release a heap of devils onto the world. You must search out 4 mysterious stones that have the power to re-seal the gate.

Players will take on the role of a soldier, Al Ted, the only survivor of a devastated militia, and they'll have to confront the evil forces that destroyed his army. The game is developed as a "survival-action RPG," and is fully polygonal, with 3D characters and backgrounds. Characters will have complete 3D movement and can dash, guard, cast spells, attack with equipped weapons, and use a combination of these actions.
