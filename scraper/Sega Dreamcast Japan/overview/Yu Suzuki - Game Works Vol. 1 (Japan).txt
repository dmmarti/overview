Yu Suzuki Game Works Vol. 1 (鈴木裕ゲームワークス Vol. 1) is a compilation of games designed by Yu Suzuki in the 1980s. It was released exclusively in Japan for the Sega Dreamcast in 2002, and came with a guide book. No second volume was ever released.

Game Selection: 
    Space Harrier
    Hang On
    Afterburner II
    Out Run
    Power Drift
