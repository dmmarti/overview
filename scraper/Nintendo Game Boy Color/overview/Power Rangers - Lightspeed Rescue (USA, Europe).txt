As a member of the Lightspeed Rescue team, players can assume the role of their favourite Power Ranger. Boys and girls will have the opportunity to rescue people from dastardly monsters, collect awesome power-ups, drive Power Ranger vehicles, battle monsters in huge arenas, search for hidden bonus levels, and even take control of a powerful Megazord! Six extraordinary heroes including Chad (Blue Ranger), Joel (Green Ranger), Carter (Red Ranger), Dana (Pink Ranger), Kelsey (Yellow Ranger) and Ryan (Titanium Ranger), are the newest team of Power Rangers dedicated to fighting the evil Diabolico and his army of relentless demons. Their mission is to protect the city of Mariner Bay. Whenever danger is around the corner, these superheroes rush to the rescue at the speed of light.
