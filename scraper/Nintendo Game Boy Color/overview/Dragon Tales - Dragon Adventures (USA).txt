In Dragon Tales: Dragon Adventures, kids are whisked away on a magical journey to Dragon Land where they embark on fun-filled adventures with such beloved characters as Emmy, Max, and the dragons Ord and Cassie. Children can join Emmy and Max as they visit the colorful Stickleback Mountains, Singing Springs, Crystal Caverns, and more. Loaded with tons of adventures, challenging tasks, and exciting rewards, Dragon Tales: Dragon Adventures allows children to experience unforgettable visits to Dragon Land for endless hours of enjoyment.
