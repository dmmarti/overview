With this game you can actually create totally new outfits for your Barbie doll using a software to edit the clothes and then printing them to actual fabric, supplied along with the game.Edit clothes for different occasions (wedding, casual, party, date, work and beach), and then choose accessories, patterns, colors, and much more.Once you're done you can watch your clothes being shown at the catwalk by Barbie models, or print photos of your creations.
