In this game, players plow roads, construct buildings, and explore the world of Tonka trucks. As players progress through the game they will receive virtual money, power-ups, and rewards that can be used to upgrade vehicles and advance through the various levels. Players will also accumulate statistics and acquire knowledge to complete more complex puzzles and contracts. Helpful characters will also pop up to offer assistance along the way. There are seven Tonka vehicles to operate, and 50 missions.
