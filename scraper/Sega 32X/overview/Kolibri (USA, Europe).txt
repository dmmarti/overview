Kolibri is a hummingbird hero in this unusual side scrolling shooter for the 32X.  Deep inside the earth's core, there is a crystal from another world.  This crystal is the earth's life force, and essential to it's survival.  One day, a dark crystal falls from the sky and damages the earth's crystal.  Desperate, the earth's crystal rockets to the surface and empowers Kolibri with the abilities he needs to defeat the dark crystal.

This game features 19 levels set in a variety of detailed natural settings.  Your goal in some levels is to destroy enemy bugs, in others your goal is to merely survive.
