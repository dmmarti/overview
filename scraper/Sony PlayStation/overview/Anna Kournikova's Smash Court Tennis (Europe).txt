A sequel to the popular Smash Court Tennis series, this time endorsed and starring Russia's tennis minx, Anna Kournikova.

Anna Kournikova is here to show you how its done. Learn all the real shots of tennis, bounce around on the multiple game modes, including Tournament, Street Tennis and the explosive Bomb Ball! 

The more games you win, the more characters appear, each with different attributes and skills. Gain extra items for your characters. Multiplayer fun for up to 4 players.
