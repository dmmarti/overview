On His way to Beach, Bugs Bunny accidentally finds and activates a time machine. Bugs Bunny is immediately propelled through five different area in time and must find his way back to the present.
It wont be an easy task, because blocking  his way back to the present are some of the wildest troublemakers(Stupid villians) he has ever known like Elmer Fudd, Witch Haxel, Yosemite Sam, Rocky and Marvin.
