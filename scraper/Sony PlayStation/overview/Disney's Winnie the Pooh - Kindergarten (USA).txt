Disney's Winnie the Pooh: Kindergarten is a hybrid title, using Macromedia Director.  Designed for kids between 3 and 6, Disney's Winnie the Pooh: Kindergarten is obviously an educational game. With Winnie the Pooh in the Forest of Blue Dreams, kids will discover nature and the four seasons, listen to musical instruments and even have the opportunity to become treasure seekers. They will also play with letters, numbers, shapes and colors.
