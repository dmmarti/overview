In the jungle everything is as usual. The old lion is a good and wise king, and the animals follow his advise. Only Robin, the King' little son doesn't always listen. Robin wants to find the treasure of the Black Panther but his father has forbidden him to look for it. Keno, the son of the Black Panther wants to find the treasure as well. Robin and Keno start as enemies but end up as best friends.
