Arthur is King of Camelot and son of Merlin and the witch, Avelon. Protector of England from the barbarian hordes, Arthur, and Queen Guinevere face a terrible challenge when the evil Mordred attacks, bringing chaos to the kingdom. Ad well as a great half-hour video, Camelot features an easy-to-use virtual colouring book and a challenging puzzle game. Can Arthur and the Knights of the Round Table save the kingdom?
