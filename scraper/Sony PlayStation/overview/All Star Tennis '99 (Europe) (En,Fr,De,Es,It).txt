Play as 8 world-class ATP/WTA tour players (including Michael Chang, Gustavo Kuerten, Mark Philippoussis, Richard Krajicek, Conchita Martínez and Jana Novotná) as well as 4 fictional players through a range of tournaments on grass, clay, hard court and rebound ace surfaces.  Strikes such as lobs, volleys, smashes are available. Multiplayer options are offered for singles and doubles: up to four players can enter the challenge in a 2-on-2 match.
