Your spaceship has fallen on a planet. As one of 4 different characters (fighter, amazone, magician) you have to find 20 flasks filled with medicine. 

Your character can find 4 weapons who can be upgraded. With these and 16 magic spells you have to fight against 20 different enemies (zombies, aliens, ghosts). Altogether O.D.T. has 7 levels (jungle, factory, kitchen) to explore. Like a RPG you can sometimes upgrade you character skills. The game has no save option but save points.
