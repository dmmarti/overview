Featuring the cast of characters from the 3D platform title, Rayman 2: The Great Escape, Rayman Rush is a collection of multiplayer arenas where the goals range from racing the 3 other characters to the finish line to a battle royal fire fight. 24 maps are available in total, and if you have the multi-tap add-on for the Playstation console, you can have up to 4 players battling it out.
