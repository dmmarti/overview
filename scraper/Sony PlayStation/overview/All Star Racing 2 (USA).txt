This is the next installment of All Star Racing. The game features 4 different game modes with each mode featuring a different vehicle type to race. There's the Bike mode featuring superbikes, the Kart mode featuring kart racing, GP mode featuring open wheeled Grand Prix cars, and a Truck mode featuring tractor-trailer trucks to race. Each of these vehicles comes in a variety of colors. Each mode features 5 different tracks to race on. Each race has the player trying to beat 5 other racers to achieve 1st place.

Options include setting the number of laps (3,6,9,12 or 15), the difficulty level (easy, medium, or hard), and whether to turn vibration on or off.
