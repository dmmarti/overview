Association football, commonly known as football or soccer, is a sport played between two teams of eleven players with a spherical ball. At the turn of the 21st century, the game was played by over 250 million players in over 200 countries, making it the world's most popular sport. The game is played on a rectangular field of grass or green artificial turf, with a goal in the middle of each of the short ends. The object of the game is to score by driving the ball into the opposing goal. Football Madness let the players feel the passion and tension of the big match atmosphere. Crunching tackles and aggressive barges are all fair play in this game. Playing without the interruption of an irritating ref, you can do what you always wanted to without being sent off. You can even resort to dirty tactics!! Football Madness is a 5 vs 5 soccer game that features 16 national teams, 4 different stadiums and 3 game modes: - League - Single Match - Cup
