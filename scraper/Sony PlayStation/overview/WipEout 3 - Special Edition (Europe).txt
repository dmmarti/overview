This special edition of Wipeout 3 (or Wip3out) has all the tracks from Wip3out, 3 from the original Wipeout and 5 from Wipeout 2097 (A.K.A. Wipeout XL) and some improvements around the original Wip3out tracks. The game menu has also been improved. I.E. the small fonts in the original Wip3out have been enlarged and the default name screen also includes numbers now.
