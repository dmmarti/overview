1997 NHL Hockey game that departed from the incremental year-to-year improvements to ice-hockey games.  Offers new AI for play against the computer that is "All-Star" level and the default game that offers honest hockey, pure and simple, crisp and satisfying.

Other features include, team management, new moves previously unseen such as hip checks, fake shots, backwards skating and more.  All 26 NHL arenas are playable with realistic player abilities based on their real NHL statistics.
