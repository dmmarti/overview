In World League Soccer '98 the player is able to move, kick, chip, volley and perform advanced passing techniques; including one-twos, chip returns and swerved long-balls. You can also chest and head the ball with total control. Also, you can alter pitch type, weather conditions, even stadium design for increased realism.

There are 8 major European leagues plus the US and Japanese leagues, with 145 teams available to choose from, as well as International competitions which can be custom designed. Live commentary is provided by Peter Brackley and Ray Wilkins and Motion Capture by Les Ferdinand.
