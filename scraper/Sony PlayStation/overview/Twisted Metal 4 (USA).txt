The fourth game in the Twisted Metal series!  The Twisted Metal tournament's boss since the beginning, Calypso, has been overthrown by the maniacal clown Sweet Tooth and his circus buddies with there wish for winning the tournament.  Now Sweet Tooth has the chance to hold his own tournament, with more powerful vehicles, tons of battleground, and a whole new selection of drivers and old legends like thumper, axel, auger and even minion and much more. The prize is the same - Sweet Tooth will grant any wish the winner asks for!  But this time, Calypso's participating in the tournament...
