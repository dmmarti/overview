Namco hits us with a collection of some of the greatest arcade games ever. Included in the package are:

1. Pac-Man
 
Pole Position
 
Galaga
 
Toy Pop
 
Rally X
2. New Rally X
3. Star Destroyer
4. Bosconian

Not only can you play these games, but you can also learn about them in the virtual museum mode included on the disc, including their history, art and the original arcade cabinets.
