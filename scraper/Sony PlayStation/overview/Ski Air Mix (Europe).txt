Master the wildest international ski slopes with the help of 4 player characters and 11 different pairs of skis. Alpen or Versus Modes allow you to challenge the computer or a friend as you race towards the bottom of the 5 slopes. Perform wild stunts and speed compete simultaneously in this great futuristic board game. The depth of a role-playing-game is coupled with the excitement of four player competition in this struggle to claim relics scattered throughout a broken 2ting you join Lord in his adventures.
