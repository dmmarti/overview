Konami Antiques MSX Collection is a series of compilations of MSX computer games released by Konami in Japan for the Sony PlayStation and Sega Saturn between 1997 and 1998. This compilation includes the classic games:
 - Konami's Golf (1984)
 - Konami's Billiards (1983)
 - Hyper Sports 3 (1985)
 - Athletic Land (1984)
 - Magical Tree (1984)
 - Yie Ar Kung-Fu II (1985)
 - Super Cobra (1983)
 - Twin Bee (1986)
 - Gradius 2 (1987)
