Hey dude! Grab your surfboard and carve up the awesome waves ranging from the perfect 5-foot peelers off Malibu Beach, to the huge reef waves off Fiji's Tavarua Island. Play with the dolphins and seagulls as you ride barrels, leap off huge waves and try to avoid that embarrassing wipe out.
