Madness on ice!  EA tries to reproduce the success of NFL Blitz with this crossover 3 on 3 Hockey / wrestling title.  Includes all of the teams and key players from the NHL as well as created teams and over the top checking, fighting and power-up skating.  Includes many unlockables and cheats, special game modes (first to 10, old time hockey) and 10 totally new arenas.
