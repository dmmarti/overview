The sequel to Bottom of the 9th, this version still continues to provide players with a full authentic MLB license including teams, their respective logos and over 700 of the major players of the time in 3D polygon graphics.

If also features the obligatory game modes such as Exhibition, Season, Championship and the like along with extensive stat tracking in numerous categories and the ability to trade players to build the ultimate "dream team".  A new feature is the "Scenario Mode" that sets the player in a make or break situation to attempt to win a game on a full-count, bottom of the ninth situation, along with many other numerous scenarios.
