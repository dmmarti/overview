Westlife Fan-O-Mania is a quiz game aimed at fans of the Irish boy-group Westlife. The game consists of 500 questions centered around the band members and their lives. If the player correctly answers all questions in one level, a video interview with one of the boys is shown. There are five levels in total presented by a hostess named Tara. A multiplayer mode lets players compete against each other to see who knows the most about the band. The game also features video snippets, a photo gallery and audio recordings of the band members.
