Starwinder is a space-based racing game, where the "road" is a twisting and turning rail which you have to stay close to in order to achieve the maximum speed.  You play as Connor Rhodes, the Earth's first emissary to the biggest race in the galaxy.  There are a variety of colorful characters from different regions of the galaxy that also participate in the race.  There are a variety of ships and weapons that are unlocked as you progress through the game.
