6 new X-Men characters join the ranks, including Rogue, Forge, Nightcrawler and Havok and two secret fighters; 6 new arenas including the X-Mansion, Muir Island, an eerily lit church and Asteroid M (Magneto's Stage) plus two secret levels; and the Cerebro mode, where you can check out behind-the-scenes character sketches, costume designs and movie set sketches from the X-Men movie, makes this sequel to Mutant Academy. Also keep an eye out for Professor X himself, who teaches you the many moves available to your character in the Academy Training mode.
