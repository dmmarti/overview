Sporting 16 fully licensed team and stadium endorsements, European Super League brings together the talent from many teams in Europe to create a 'European Super League Championship'.  PRODUCT FEATURES: •Entries from clubs such as AC Milan, Bayern Munich, Marseille, Liverpool, Chelsea and Paris Saint Germain   •Stadiums are modelled to real-life specifications   •Control interface when played in novice mode is limited to simple button combinations, allowing for a newcomer to play the game more easily; for the advanced gamer, the alternate mode, although more complex and demanding, will give them improved control over the game   •Authentic crowd chants
