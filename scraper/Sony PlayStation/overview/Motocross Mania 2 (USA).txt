Motocross Mania 2 is a motorcycle racing game for 1 up to 2 players. The game contains arcade mode (race and time attack practice), career mode with three difficulty levels and track editor. Apart from winning of races the player may perform a variety of tricks. The game allows to choose the gender of the racer, one of five motorbikes and one of twelve courses. The player has the ability to change the settings of the motorcycle, such as suspension, exhaust, brakes. The gameplay is typical for arcade racing games, the motorbike can't be damaged, although the player may fall down. The game features a multiplayer mode on a split screen - horizontal or vertical.
