Dog Fight is a military aircraft dog fighting game. The player controls a small aircraft, which can either circle left, circle right, or fly straight ahead. The plane is also armed with a small cannon which can fire straight ahead. The goal is to eliminate all enemy aircraft on the screen. The player can customize the number of enemy aircraft. When a plane is shot down, a small pilot with a parachute will emerge, and float slowly to the bottom of the screen. Includes a demonstration mode.</Notes
