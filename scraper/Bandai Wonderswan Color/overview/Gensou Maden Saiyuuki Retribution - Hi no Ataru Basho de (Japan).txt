Utopia, Togenkyo where humans and youkai coexist. An incident occurred in Togenkyo, and the youkai became violent and began to attack humans. In order to prevent the rebirth of the bull demon king who was the cause of this incident, the gods of the heavens ordered the four men, the Sanzo Houshi, Son Goku, Sago Jorge, and Hachihachi Command to stop the rebirth of the bull demon king.

Players must choose only one partner from the four sisters, use their daughters and Sanzos to make money at the stalls, and make money for the accommodation. Can you grow your sales steadily and earn your rent safely? In addition, during the game, mini-games such as ?card game?, ?monster extermination game? and ?puzzle game? are also hidden.
