Based on the events of the Three Kingdoms era in China (3rd century), and on Luo Guanzhong's famous novel Sanguo Yanyi, you take command of one of the many warlords rivaling for control after the Yellow Turban Rebellion.

This strategy/RPG game introduces hundreds of historically real characters of the Three Kingdoms era. The main purpose of the game is to be supreme ruler of the remnants of the falling Han Dynasty. To do this you have to build a mighty empire by conquering provinces and recruiting generals worthy of command. Fight massive battles, triumph over natural disasters and capture the enemy stronghold is the name of the game. In the end, can you unite China?
