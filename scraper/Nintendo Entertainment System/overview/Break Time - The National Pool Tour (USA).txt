Time to "rack 'em up" in this late release from FCI. Choose one of four different games - Rotation, 8-ball, 9-ball, and 14-1 Rack Game - each based on professional pool rules. As if that wasn't enough, also included are Practice and Watch Modes and the "National Pool Tour," where you must test your skills against five different pros in five different cities on your way to becoming the champion. Features digitized commentary and password save.
