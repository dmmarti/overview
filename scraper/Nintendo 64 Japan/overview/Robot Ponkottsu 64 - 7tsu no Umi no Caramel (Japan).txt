Robot Ponkottsu 64: Nanatsu no Umi no Caramel (Robot Ponkottsu 64: Caramel of the Seven Seas) is a role-playing video game for the Nintendo 64. It was released only in Japan, in 1999. It is part of the Robopon series of games, and can make use of the Transfer Pak with Robot Ponkottsu Sun, Star and Moon Versions.
