The nations nuclear strike capability is no longer under the control of the united states government. The destructive thermonuclear force of 10,000 warheads has been hijacked by a madman!

Elvin Atombender is a bonafide genius, a technological wizard, a terminally ill criminal mastermind who is trying to carve his name in the history books with a nuclear firestorm. This lunatic using a powerful electromutative computer virus has not only infected 90% of the nation's computers but has reprogrammed the national defense computers to destroy several major cities within 24 hours.

Now it's up to you. You must pentrate his headquarters. The gigantic starcom international building and search for information and weapons. Use stealth and cunning to outwit his advanced robotic security force. It will take more than guts to navigate the maze of elevators and passage ways that link the eight towers of elvin's stronghold.
