Dizzy Prince of Yolkfolk


"Battle Rockwart the Troll and meet Dizzyis new friend Pogie the Fluffle in the latest game. Summon King Arthur from his crusade and wake Daisy from her hundred year snooze!"

In this game, Dizzy needs to rescue Daisy from the evil troll called Rockwort, who has put her to sleep and locked her up in his castle. You must find a way to reach her by burning down doors, completing tasks and being knighted.
