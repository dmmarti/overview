	In this tennis simulation, the player is Spanish tennis hero Emilio Sanchez Vicario. The player can play tennis against the computer or against a friend in two player mode.    It is possible to play just one match or a tournament. In tournament mode the player can select which grand slam to play- the Australia Open, Roland Garros, Wimbledon, and the US Open.    The player can select their opponent from a list of famous tennis players, such as Lendl, Becker, Wilander, Edberg, or McEnroe.    The game follows standard tennis rules and allows the player to hit a variety of forehand and backhand shots to any location on the court.
