Wembley Greyhounds is a betting game that allows one to three players to bet on various dog races over a season on various courses. As money is won or lost on each race, a scoreboard is used to show the progress of each player with the aim to finish top at the end of the season. Each dog that wins goes through to Wembley to compete to be champion dog.

Before a race, each player has various options like looking at tables and stats, but the main options allow the players to bet. There are two types of bookies to be chosen and they are a choice of four bookies to select or the course bookie. As decisions are made, a timer counts down to the race and the odds of the dogs keep changing. There are three types of bets that can be made and they are win only, the first two dogs in the correct order, and the first two dogs in any order.
