	In "Goody" you are a thief with a mission to break into a Spanish bank. For you need the appropriate tools, such as a torch and pincers. In your road toward the bank, you must avoid policemen, gorillas, kung-fu experts, women sweeping and paper airplanes... that is to say, the typical problems to those that any thief would face. The game is an arcade adventure of those of using the correct object in the correct place, similar to Jet Set Willy.
