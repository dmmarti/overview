Trivial Pursuit: Young Players Edition is the first add-on question pack for Domark's Trivial Pursuit: Genus Edition designed for children and teens. This adds an additional 3,000 trivia questions separated into 2 age groups: 7 thru 12 and 12+. Original categories Sport and Leisure, Science and Nature, and Entertainment are replaced with People and Places, the Nature World and Games and Hobbies.
