Brick Breaker is a Breakout variant consisting of a Classic Mode and a Dual Paddle Mode.

The Classic Mode contains fifty different levels to play through. Players bounce a ball off a paddle trying to get the ball to destroy all the bricks on a level. Sometimes a destroyed brick will drop a power-up. Power-ups include things such as a rocket launcher, glue that stick the ball to the paddle, multi-balls, and hearts that give the player an extra life.
