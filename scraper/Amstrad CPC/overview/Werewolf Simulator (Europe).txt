You are Tommy Astle, an experienced reporter for the local city newspaper and your Editor has just tasked you to investigate a few mysterious deaths. There has been talk of the supernatural and a mythical creature called The loogaroo and off course you are sceptical, but the further you investigate, the more you become a believer. Will you get to the bottom of these deaths and the creature or is it all just a myth Werewolf Simulator is a graphics text adventure where there is text describing your surroundings with a picture of what you see. You type in commands to explore further into the game.
