Muggins the Spaceman is a graphic action adventure where the objective is for Muggins to recover a new wonder spaceship that crashed during a meteorite shower and bring it back to dock for a proper repair.

In order to be able to fly the ship to the dock, Muggins has to find all the parts that were distributed all over during the crash and put them in their correct place. However the security system is still active and hostile security robots roam the ship. Holes open constantly on the hull that need to be repaired, if too many holes are left unpatched the ship will be destroyed.
