In this top view racing game, your are driving around in a town with a maze-like road infrastructure. Your goal is to collect the white flags while you are chased by hostile cars that will try to crash you head-on. A round is completed once three flags have been collected.  
 
When driving you will find several items as money bags and fuel barrels which can be picked up.  
 
To put the picked up fuel barrels to use, and to refill your gas tank you 'll have to drive to the gas station.
