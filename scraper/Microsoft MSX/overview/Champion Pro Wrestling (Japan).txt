A straightforward Pro Wrestling game from Sega in the Champion sport series. You always start at 
rank C and have to fight your way up to rank B and A.  
To play for a rank you 'll have to fight three matches. Winning two out of three matches promotes you to an higher rank. 
During a match several moves can be selected: chop, kick, pile driver, dropkick.  
The referee does a countdown when your opponent or you are down.
