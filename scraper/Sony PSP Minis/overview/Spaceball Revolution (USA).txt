Spaceball: Revolution will put your intelligence and skill to the test as you solve puzzles in a futuristic environment. You have to copy the figures that appear on the top screen, using energy balls that make the boxes used to construct the figure correctly change color. But it won't be an easy task... Different obstacles will be thrown into your path, making you polish your aim and think rapidly. Fifteen different levels are waiting for you in "Spaceball: Revolution." Are you up to the challenge? Only the very best players manage to reach the end!
