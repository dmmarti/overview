Welcome to Twisted Dragon Media's debut title, M.O.Z.O.X. Space Salvager, are you up for the challenge? A high action arcade style space shooter with infinite levels of play. Use a combination of 4 weapons, and high maneuverability to dodge, weave, and blast your way to the next level. In each level of play you will eliminate enemy ships, weave your way through astroid fields, and outsmart a boss ship. Collect special weapons, shields, debris orbs(points), and multipliers along the way to improve your chances to get a high score.
