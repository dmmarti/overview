 In half wrecked Atolls, the last remaining Atollers are waiting to be rescued. As the Mariner, your mission is to prevent Smokers from enslaving them. The Smokers most prized target is a little girl named Enola, who has a map to dry land tattooed on her back. In the game, Enola is the floating Atoller that flashes. If you are able to prevent Enola from being captured, at the end of the round the bonus multiplier will increase-quoted from instruction manual
