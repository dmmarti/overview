 Kirby is a an interesting little super-hero.  Although he appears to be a weak little marshmallow, he is actually quite powerful.  He has the ability to suck up his enemies like a vacuum, and then shoot them out at other enemies.  Kirby can also swallow air, allowing him to fly through the air.  In this game, Kirby must save his land from the evil King Dedede.  There are 4 levels of side-scrolling platform action.
