 WWF Superstars is a licensed wrestling game. Player can  choose between 5 WWF superstars: Ultimate Warrior, Hulk Hogan, Macho King Randy Savage, Mr. Perfect and the Million Dollar Man to take on opponents in the ring.  There are a lot of moves to choose from: body slam, suplex, clothes line and many more as well as signature moves for each of the superstars. Two players can compete against each other via the Game Boy Gamelink.
