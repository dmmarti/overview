Springster offers the finest in high resolution, brilliant color graphics.  Maneuver through 32 different, exciting mazes in search of the Maze Melon.  Battle bad guys, collect treasures and race the clock during your perilous quest.  Three skill levels insures fun for all members of the family.  One or two players allowed.

Required Equipment: Color computer 3 with 128K RAM, television or RGB monitor, joysticks
