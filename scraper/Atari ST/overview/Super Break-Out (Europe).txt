Super Break-Out is a break-out game in high resolution for one player.  The game features 40 different bricks and many power-ups/bonuses and several types of monsters. Bonuses are marked with letters or numbers and will fall down if you hit the right brick. Among them you may find two or five extra balls, lots of bonus points and kill all the monsters.  You get 100 points for every normal brick you hit, 2500 points for completing a level, 400 points for each bonus you catch and 100 points for every monster you kill.  When you lost all your bats you are given the choice to either continue at the last level, start new at the beginning or quit With the included level editor anyone can make his/her own levels.
