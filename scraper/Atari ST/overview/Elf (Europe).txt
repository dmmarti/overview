Elf (not be confused with Elf from Ocean Software) is 2D side-scrolling action game in fantasy world. The player controls a green-suited elf with bow and arrows, who must find 2 halves of golden pumpkins. He explores big levels, avoid traps, like spikes or holes (elf dies of a fall) and shoots various enemies, like skeletons giant flies or small humanoid creatures. One shot/contact with enemy and Elf loses life. He has limited arrows, but he can find new packs. The game has 8 levels.
