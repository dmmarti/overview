Conqueror is a turn based strategy game for up to six players. Every turn consists of three phases:Reinforce armies Move armies. Attack enemies. To control a square you need to have an army at that square, an army with minimum strength will do. Armies can be moved one square at a time but you cam keep moving them and conquer land until it's strength reaches zero. The game ends when there is only one player left. The game comes with a map editor and 8 ready made maps. A typical map consists of 40x24 squares and wraps horizontally. Land square are grey and water squares light blue.
