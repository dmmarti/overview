Frontline is a squad-level tactical strategy game set in many fronts of the World War 2. The included scenarios are Monte Cassino 1944, Guadalcanal 1942, Stalingrad 1943, Pegasus Bridge 1944 and two tutorial missions. Walls and shell holes may provide cover on the varied battlefields.  The game features an icon-driven menu-based interface.
