Super League Soccer is a football manager game. Start with a team in division 4 and try to make it to the top of the league. The game is menu driven, the main menu gives the player access to the next match, the league table, the squad menu, the transfer market, the economy menu, the training menu and other options. The training menu gives the player the possibility to train different parts of the team or the whole team. The only economy options is to take or repay loans. In the transfer market the player may buy new team members. The name of the players and teams is customizable.
