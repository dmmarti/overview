Black Jack Plus 3 is a highly customizable Black Jack game in medium resolution for up to seven players. Computer players can have one of seven strategies and those strategies can be edited in the strategies window. There you can set options like max bet, insurance yes/no, etc, etc. Human players can play all by them selves or with feedback from the computer. Four different house rules can be edited. The rules include what to do when getting 17, when the dealer should be able to use double down and if the dealer should be able to surrender.  You can play with 1-9 decks and a game can consist of up to 999999 deals. The gameplay can be logged to file or printer.
