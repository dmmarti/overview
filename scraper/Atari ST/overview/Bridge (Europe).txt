Bridge It is a board game for one player coded in GFA BASIC 3.5. The object of the game is to get from the start to the finish before the computer. The human player and the computer takes turns rolling a die and moving their piece accordingly, rolling a six means an extra roll. Scattered around the board is colored squares. Two squares of the same color are connected by a bridge. I you land on a colored square you automatically travel across the bridge to the other side, this might not always be a good thing. If you roll a six and land on a colored square you are not affected by it.
