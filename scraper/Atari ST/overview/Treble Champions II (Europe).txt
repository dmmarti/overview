Treble Champions II is a managerial simulation in which the player takes over a team in the fifth UK division. The goal is to become successful in the Premier League in the long run. In comparison to the Treble Champions the options, previously restricted to training, lineup and transfer market, were expanded: now the player also may change the ticket prices, expand the stadium, borrow money from the bank and set up friendly matches before the season, Every athlete has a number of stats. There is no match presentation except a statistics field which gets updated over time.
