Fruit Machine is as can be expected a simulation of slot machine. The game is for one player who controls the machine with the keyboard. There are four reels and the player has to get three of four fruits in a row to win. After a win it's possible to gamble the earnings by choosing if a number is higher or lower than the that is about to appear. The fruits on the reels have number on them and if their sum is ten or greater the player reaches Club Casino. This give access to features and the higher the numbers the better feature is available. To get a better feature it's possible to gamble. If the player gets a question mark he is given a bonus chance which can be additional Club Casino numbers, a free respin or a skill chance.
