You are a reporter for a local newspaper with financial problems who must try to find that big scoop that will push you into the big times and help your financial woes. Waking up in the morning, you must go through a morning routine, leave the house and get to your workplace. Once at work your boss will give you various jobs and you must investigate these and hope one will be the big scoop you are looking for. Scoop! is a text adventure with text on the screen describing your location and you use the keyboard to type in commands to interact with each location and move to the next one.
