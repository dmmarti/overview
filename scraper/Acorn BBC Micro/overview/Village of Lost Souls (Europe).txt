12 Lost Souls is an Adventure game, developed and published by Len Townsend, which was released in Europe in 1987.

In the world of Albion only magic can be used by people with The Talent and controlled by the Order of Leofric. There is a cloud of Doom as you Nathan, an inquisitor go to investigate Lord Talent of Dinham who is attempting to open a portal into the Thirteenth Realm which brings chaos and disorder. You must find him and stop him bringing a Realm Of Chaos to Albion.

Realm of Chaos: Village of Lost Souls is a text only adventure where text on the screen describes your surroundings as you enter commands using the keyboard to complete your adventure.
