 Grandma Rose has won $488 millions by playing at the lottery. The only problem she has is that she lost her ticket! So, she hired you, one of the most famous investigators. You're the Mystery P.I. and you're decided to help her by trying to retrace her footsteps.  Mystery P.I.: The Lottery Ticket is a seek and find game, meaning that you have to search for hidden objects. At the beginning of each level, you're given a list of what you have to find in a limited time. Once you spot an item on the list, you have to click on it. There are also mini-games. The game features also ranks. The "Perfect Investigation" gives a big bonus of points, making you rising through the ranks faster.
