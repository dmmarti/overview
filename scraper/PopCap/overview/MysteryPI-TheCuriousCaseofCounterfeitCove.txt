 Whaler's Cove is preparing for the fall tourism season, but counterfeit money is popping up everywhere.  You've been hired by the town elders to track down the counterfeiting ring before the fake bills ruin the local economy.  Visit 25 charming seaside locations as you search for more than 2,200 hidden objects. Explore more than 100 levels and find hidden lobsters and keys to unlock 2 bonus game modes. Solve 6 unique mini-game puzzles. Go for a Perfect Search and earn speed bonuses to beat your high score.
