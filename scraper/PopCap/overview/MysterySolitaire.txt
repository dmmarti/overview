 Mystery Solitaire: Secret Island is a solitaire card game with a storyline.  You are cast away on a secret island and are trying to solve a mystery. In order to do this, visit 12 spots on the island to search for clues. In order to reach the various investigation spots, you must play several rounds of solitaire in which you match pairs of playing cards from an elaborate spread. Once you reach the seek and find round, the game challenges you to find a list of objects contained within the scene in order to uncover the clue.
