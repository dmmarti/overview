Cowboy Bebop: Tsuioku no Serenade (カウボーイビバップ 追憶の夜曲(せれなーで), Kaubōi Bibappu: Tsuioku no Serenēdo, lit. "Cowboy Bebop: Serenade of Reminiscence") is a PlayStation 2 action/beat 'em up video game from Bandai, released exclusively in Japan on August 25, 2005. It has an original story based on the anime series Cowboy Bebop.</Notes
