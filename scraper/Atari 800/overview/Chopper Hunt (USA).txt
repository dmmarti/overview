You're a sometime geologist, pilot and demolitions expert - a legendary soldier of fortune - who has stumbled upon a treasure map.  Venturing deep into the Bad Lands, your scanners clatter wildly.  Eureka! You've found priceless objects buried deep in the earth.  One Problem, though: the treasure lies within a nuclear waste dump site.  Blast through to the treasures, retrieve them and make it back to your base before time and fuel run out.  And watch out!  Hovering aircraft start filling in craters as soon as you make them.
