Racing game thrills meet platformer charm in MotoHeroz, a side-scrolling racer built with simple controls and engaging physics. The result is a new kind of racing adventure! Players race, chase, and rally their way through a brightly colored world, competing against their friends for the fastest times, entering daily races, and hunting for secrets and coins.     Features    Rich single player Story Adventure  Two to four player Party Rally local multiplayer  Daily online competitions with Leader Boards  100+ tracks, 14 cars, and 3 game modes = tons of fun!
