FISHIE FISHIE, the one button game.   Welcome to Fishie Fishie, a game that can be played with just one button. You re going to experience something extraordinary that will really test your reflexes. The game contains a series of tutorials that will teach you not only how to control your fishie, but also the various game modes. In Fishie Fishie, you are a fish that has to eat all the time. You also have to finish several different game modes as quickly as possible. Every second counts. To win, you ll have to get through over a hundred levels spread over seven game modes:and a large competition. You can play solo, or up to four people can play on the same screen. In co-op mode or every fishie for himself, it s no holds barred to win.
