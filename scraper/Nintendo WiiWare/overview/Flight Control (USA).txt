Control a never-ending stream of aircraft in this insanely addictive worldwide hit from Firemint! Take on the role of an air traffic controller at an increasingly busy airport. Direct all manner of aircraft to safety, but don t let them collide!    Sounds easy? As the planes roll in thick and fast, you ll need nerves of steel and smart strategy to keep the chaos under control. To play, just use your Wii Remote to select and drag planes to their landing zones. You can even work with friends and family in instant drop-in/drop-out multiplayer mode for up to four people!    Travel to five different airports including the Australian Outback and an aircraft carrier. Watch out for 10 unique aircraft types, including jets, seaplanes and helicopters. Flight Control takes only a minute to learn. Just don t expect to put it down as quickly as you pick it up!
