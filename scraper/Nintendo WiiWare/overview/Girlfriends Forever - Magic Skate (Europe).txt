The Girlfriends dream of being Queens of the Ice Rink.  If they can master all the challenges they can win the Golden Magic Skate and the Crown of the Skating Queen.  Can you help them to fulfill their dreams?    Select one of the Girlfriend characters and help her to skate through various wonderful ice rinks and other magical places. As you strive for the Crown you can collect fabulous outfits and accessories along the way.
