Remember the puppet whose nose grew? Remember the values taught by the story? Friendship, cunning and tenderness... Will you reach the end of the story of Pinocchio through a series of puzzles?     Based on the story by Carlo Collodi, Pinocchio s Puzzle is a game in which you must solve the different, progressively more difficult puzzles as you advance in the plot. Aside from developing sensory skills and hand-eye coordination, it adds the values of an endearing and mythical story told puzzle by puzzle.     Features    Enjoy with 4 friends or family members playing at once.  Puzzles progressively more difficult.  Helps develop sensory skills.  Fabulous, beautiful illustrations and orchestral music
