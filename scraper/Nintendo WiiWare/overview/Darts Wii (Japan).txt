  "DARTS Wii" is a darts game where everyone can enjoy darts easily.   The game operation is similar to the actual dart s play style. Hold the Wii remote control so that you have an arrow of darts, and shake the Wii remote to the screen.   Three of the representative games "01", "CRICKET" and "COUNT-UP" that rules are played well and practice mode are prepared.   Each game can play from 1 to 4 people.
