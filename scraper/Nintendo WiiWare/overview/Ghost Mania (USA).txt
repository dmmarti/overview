Ghost Mania is an original puzzle game for players of all ages that gives a new twist to falling block games. Form and expand rows of ghosts and blocks of any size in any direction!     The game focuses on the misadventures of Becky and Tim, two ghost keepers who just lost their job. After falling asleep in hours of work, they have accidentally let out all the ghosts of the World of Spirits! As a result, Becky and Tim have been banished until they manage to capture all the ghosts again.     Help Becky and Tim get their jobs back so they can return to the World of Spirits. Catch the fugitive ghosts before it s too late!
