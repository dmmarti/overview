Hold It!    Phoenix Wright is back, as the second game in the popular Ace Attorney handheld franchise comes to WiiWare! As Phoenix Wright, you ll play through four shocking new cases in first person perspective! The game features two unique game play modes. In Investigations Mode you ll survey crime scenes, interview witnesses and gather evidence. You ll then present findings, listen to testimonials and examine witnesses in Court Mode. Justice For All also includes a new Psyche-Lock feature that allows Phoenix to use a line of questioning to reveal inconsistencies in witness testimony.
