You probably know exactly what video games are and how to play them. However, if you haven t played the iFun4all s FishTank yet, you can t really say that you have seen everything!     FishTank is a combination of a very popular Match 3 genre with a puzzle video gamestyle. It is an arcade-style matching game but instead of matching gems or blocks you collect shoals of colored fish. In order to do that, you need to build up a combination of four or more identical fish and press a corresponding button to remove them from the net. The gameplay goes horizontally . not vertically as you might be used to.     Looking for more? Here we go:     - 24 achievements to unlock  - 24 challenging levels  - 9 different power-ups to enhance your playing experience  - 4 player co-op mode  - 2 game modes: Play and Challenges  - 1 extra ultimate trophy at the end of the game for the best gold medals collectors!     Don t try to play more than your doctor allows you! iFun4All warns: FishTank is highly addictive!
