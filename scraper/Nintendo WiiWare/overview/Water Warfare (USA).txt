Soak your friends in this family-friendly first-person shooter! Go against up to 8 players online!        Water Warfare is a comical first person shooter that uses water guns to battle and soak your opponents. Each map, a Playground, Beach, Plaza, or Nature Park theme, is generated automatically when the game is on, where you can think up new strategies each time you engage! Game modes include Single Player Missions, CPU Match, and 2-Player Split Screen.
