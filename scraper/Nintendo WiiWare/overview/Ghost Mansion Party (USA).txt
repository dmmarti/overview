A fun and frightening adventure for the whole family. What treasures will you discover inside the mansion?  Fazzy the ghost has been locked out of his mansion by some evil spooks, and he s willing to give his treasure to anyone who can chase them out! Work cooperatively and competitively with friends and family to defeat the ghosts and discover the huge treasure hidden within. Travel around this wild mansion in a board game fashion, landing on mini-games that everyone can enjoy. You ll even engage in challenges on the board itself as you try to collect magic scrolls and hints to reveal the secret word that will open the doors to the treasure. Get your Wii Remote controllers ready, as you re about to play with them in ways you ve never imagined before as you take your journey through the mansion! It s so much fun, it s scary!
