While down a big mountain of ice, the player must follow the ugly route with trees, there will be many obstacles in your way, like trees that were not cut, and snow leopards, which will cross the road running. Stay tuned for when a yellow stripe appears in the snow, this indicates that an avalanche area is nearby, so you should skip to not be swallowed by the snow.Ski Run is a fast and with good graphics game, maybe your only problem is the difficulty of the game, which does not change much as the player passes the stages, making the game a bit drab
