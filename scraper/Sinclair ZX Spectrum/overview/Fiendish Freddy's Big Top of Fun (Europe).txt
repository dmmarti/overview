	In this game, you try to save your circus from Fiendish Freddy buying it out by preforming 6 circus acts which include...    High Diving,  Juggling,  Trapeze,  Knife Throw,  Tightrope, and  Cannonball    These games are rather tricky, and watch out for Fiendish Freddy who has a fiendish trick up his sleeve every time.
