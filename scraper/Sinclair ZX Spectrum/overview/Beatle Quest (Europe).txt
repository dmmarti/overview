	Made with a program called The Quill, a text-adventure creation program, this game's story is based on a number of Beatles song lyrics.  Set in the year 2953, you are a Keeper of the Archives who takes care of Ancient Earth knowledge while space pioneers look for a New Earth to colonize. In your research, you come upon 'The Four Kings of EMI' and being fascinated you enter a computer simulation of a Beatles influenced world in 1960's Liverpool.    Through 150 locations you will adventure encountering   people and places from Beatles songs such as Maxwell who has a silver hammer, and Penny Lane.  Make sure to grab any food and drink as you go along as you must keep your health up.
