You've been left in charge of Bridgeton Asylum while the manager is away and you have messed up big time. You forgot to lock the main door and all the patients have gone out into the big bright world. You need to find them and round them all up before the manager returns and you get fired. Trouble at Bridgeton is a text adventure where text describes your location and any items of interest. You type in commands to control your player with the keyboard.
