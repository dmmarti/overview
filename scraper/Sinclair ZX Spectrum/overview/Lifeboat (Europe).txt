Lifeboat Adventure is a text adventure where the game claims to be based on a true story about the clipper Alana which hit a submerged reef of the north east coast and sank leaving just five survivors from 82 on August 10th 1927. You play the role of one of the survivors sat in a Lifeboat with four other people, Jake, Pedro, Shamus and a woman. You must interact with the other people as well as try to get the boat safely to land while trying to stay alive. The screen has text which describes your location and you type in commands with the keyboard to interact with your surroundings.
