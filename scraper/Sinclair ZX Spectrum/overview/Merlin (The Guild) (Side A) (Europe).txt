You are Merlin, a high magician in the realm of King Arthur and with all the Knights of the Round Table away in a tournament, it has been left for you to complete an important quest. king Arthur's wife, Guinevere has been kidnapped and taken to Boscastle. Using your magic you must search the surrounding areas finding additional spells and equipment to aid you in your quest. Merlin is a two-part text adventure where the screen has text describing your location and you type in commands with the keyboard to interact with your surroundings.
