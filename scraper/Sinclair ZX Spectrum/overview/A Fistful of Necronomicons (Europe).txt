 A Fistful Of Necronomicons is a single player, humorous, text adventure in which the player takes the role of Brian Woodlouse, a friend of Rodney Prentice a wizard's assistant. Rodney got bored with his work around the wizard's place of work so he started reading the Necronomicon. This was a bad idea because the book made Rodney cast a teleport spell and now it's gone.Luckily the wizard is away so Rodney has asked for your help in collecting the ingredients for a homing spell This game was developed using Professional Adventure Writer. It supports the standard shortcuts such as N/S/E/W for North/South/East/West
