After three months at sea, you Jolly Jack have been given shore leave for the night and your aim is to get drunk, have sex with a woman before getting safely back to your ship. You have 50 pounds in your pocket and you have to explore the town, Sharksville to fulfill your needs. Jolly Jack's Run Ashore is a adventure where text on the screen describes your surroundings and you type in commands with the keyboard to interact with your location.
