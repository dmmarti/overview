	Phantomas is a Jet Set Willy influenced platform game consisting of 80 rooms. The task is to reach a millionaire's mansion and 'borrow' as much as possible from it. First you must reach the mansion by passing through a junkyard, then negotiate the palace and its maze of traps, then escape through underground caverns. You have a single life, contact with enemies will drain this gradually, but squares of energy can be collected en route. You get transport between levels in different ways, including rockets and helicopters.
