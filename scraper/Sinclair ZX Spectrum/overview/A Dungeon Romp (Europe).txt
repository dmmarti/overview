 Dungeon Romp is a single-player, humorous text adventure in which the player takes the role of Bernard Morris, aka Brainbeater. Bernard challenged his friend Malcolm to a drinking contest, the bet being that the loser would explore the local dungeons. After seventeen pints Bernard slumps to the floor knowing that all is lost. The next day he packs his adventurer's backpack and sets off. This game was developed using Professional Adventure Writer. It supports the standard shortcuts such as N/S/E/W for North/South/East/West as well as more advanced commands which allow the player to give up to five commands to anyone who will accept them, the manual has the following example 'SAY TO HAROLD "GO NORTH, BUY THE PAPER BUT DON'T READ IT. SOUTH AND TAKE THE DOG FOR A WALK."
