	In Super Spy a jet fighter carrying a nuclear warhead has has disappeared under mysterious circumstances while out on a training exercise and no wreckage has been found. The player has been summoned by the Spy Master and given a mission to find the jet fighter and defuse the deadly cargo.    Super Spy starts as a text adventure game in which the player is a James Bond style character who has to negotiate their way past the people and the puzzles to achieve the objectives. Once the players has found their way far enough the game changes into 3D graphic maze for the player to find their way to the goal and complete the mission.
