	Are you willing to plow through six levels of asteroids, alien ships and defense installations, and finally a Dicator's Lair, so as to save the sleepy English town of Watford? If not, you might be better to pass on this Scramble-inspired shoot 'em up.    The caverns you fly through have the standard zig-zag walls, which must be avoided. There are no mid-level restart points, and only 3 lives, which makes things tougher
