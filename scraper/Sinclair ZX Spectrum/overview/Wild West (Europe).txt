You have lost Fort Snake to Big Nose Bill and his gang, and you are now seeking revenge and to get your fort back. Problem is, you are now stranded in the desert with no one or anything around to help. You must search the desert and surrounding areas to get to Big Nose Bill and your fort. Wild West is a graphic adventure and with the aid of three sprites giving advice, you must choose the correct advice on every screen. The top of the screen has a 1st person perspective off your surroundings while the bottom half is where the sprites are. Using a joystick you push left, right or down to bring up each ones advice. Pressing the fire button selects that sprite's advice. Push up with the joystick and you get a percentage of the game completed.
