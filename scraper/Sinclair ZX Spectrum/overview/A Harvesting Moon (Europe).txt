 Visiting Moreton Manor, an old country mansion serving as a tourist attraction, you as a youngster are separated from your parents during a visit. The objective is to reunite with your parents before the manor closes at 5pm. All is not as it seems at the manor, paranormal activity is afoot and you have to determine what is going on in the house and the spacious grounds. The courtyard clock ticks on relentlessly while you try to unlock the manor's secrets and find mother and father. A Harvesting Moon is a traditional text adventure made using the Quill adventure generation software from Gilsoft. You tell the game what you want to do via a textual interface and the parser is based on verb / noun input.
