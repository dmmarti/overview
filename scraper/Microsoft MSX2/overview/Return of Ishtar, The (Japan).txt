The Return of Ishtar is a sequel to The Tower of Druaga. It is an action game with light puzzle-solving elements that requires you to control two different characters. The characters are maiden Ki who fights with magic and the other one is Gilgamesh, the sword-wielding knight. Search for keys and special items. The tower has a few different ways to exit, and the aggregate total of levels is 128.    Players are presented a password to allow them to continue from where they left off.
