 World Sports Competition is a Summer Olympics title, originally released on the PC Engine (TurboGrafx-16).  Events include:•Archery •Field - Broad Jump, Triple Jump, High Jump, Discus Throw, Hammer Throw and Javelin Throw •Rowing •Shooting - Clay pigeon and pistol target shooting. •Swimming - Back Stroke, Breast Stroke, Butterfly, Free Style and Individual Medley. •Track - 100 Meter Dash, 110 Meter Hurdles and 400 Meter Dash.
