 Digital Champ is a boxing game where you have to fight various other fighters to become champ. The game is viewed from the perspective of your fighter with your gloves in front of your face. The other fighter is stood in front of you. The joypad is used and can enable you to dodge punches, throw various punches, use your super punch and defend your face. As well as the main playing area you also can see your stamina and energy bars, the other fighters bars and a timer.
