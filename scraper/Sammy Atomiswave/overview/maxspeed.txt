Maximum Speed is a driving game which includes a single player mode, and a 2 Player Head to Head mode.    Maximum Speed features 6 different courses, and allows players to select either automatic or manual transmission. There are 3 different difficulty levels available to the player, which ensures that Maximum Speed remains enjoyable and appealing to a variety of players.    Players can also choose from 3 distinctly different racing styles: Stock Car, Open Wheel Cart or Pickup Truck. Maximum Speed also features great 3D action graphics and steering wheel feedback, which both add a degree of realism of the game.
