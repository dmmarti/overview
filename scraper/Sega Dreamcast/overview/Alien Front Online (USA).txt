 Alien Front Online is a tank driving shoot-'em up that focuses on online play. The premise is a classic alien invasion of earth. Non-friendly aliens descend to the earth and put up strongholds around globe. You can choose to fight the resulting battles from the side of the army or the side of the aliens.  In online mode, this game supports 4 player vs. 4 player battles.   In single player mode, there are 30 missions for each side. You move from mission to mission in a branching storyline with multiple endings.
