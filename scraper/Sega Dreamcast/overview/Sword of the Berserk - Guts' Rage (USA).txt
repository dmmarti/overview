 Based on popular Japanese manga, Sword of the Berserk: Guts' Rage is an action-adventure with a medieval fantasy setting. A mysterious plague-like evil is revealed in the course of the one-eyed warrior Gattsu's personal quest to cure his love Casca of her demented state and exact revenge on the evil God Hand warriors responsible for their fate.  The story unfolds in between gory action sequences in which Gattsu uses his 7-foot-long sword and other assorted weapons to battle with countless and varied foes.
