 Sega Extreme Sports is a multi-Trial sports game where you combine different sports in one race (Snowboard, ATV, Delta-plane and Mountain Bike + 2 sports to unlock: Base Jump and Bungee Jumping).  You have all basic Game mode available: Championship, Race, Time Trial, Practice. There's three track at the beginning and six more to unlock.  The gameplay is quite basic with not much difference between the sports nevertheless you can make some simple tricks during jumps that fill your boost gauge. The difficulty is relatively hard.
