 Suzuki Alstare Extreme Racing is the sequel to Redline Racer. It now features an official Suzuki Alstare Racing Team license.  It's an arcade superbike racing game. Game modes include Main Game, Reverse Challenge and Single Race. In Main Game you have to have to compete in challenges to earn a place in the Suzuki Alstare Racing Team. Reverse Challenge is the same but with mirrored circuits.
