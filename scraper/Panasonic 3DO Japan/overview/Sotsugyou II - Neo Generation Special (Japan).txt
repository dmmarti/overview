The game is the second one in the Sotsugyou game series (the third one was Wedding Bell). In the game the player takes the role of a young teacher that got a new job in a prestigious girls private high school (Tsinghua Girls High) and got to help the girls to get their graduation that will allow them to go to university. The player can choose what activities they will do during the week, and he can go with any of them in different events in the weekends if the girl agree to a date. Depending on the player choices during the game, the girls stats at the end of the game and the relation that they got with the player's character the game ending will be one or other, since the game features different endings.
