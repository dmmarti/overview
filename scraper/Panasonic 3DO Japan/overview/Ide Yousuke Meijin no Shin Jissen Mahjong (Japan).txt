Ide Yohsuke Meijin no Shin Jissen Mahjong is a mahjong game hosted by Ide Yohsuke. The player can participate in different types of mahjong tournaments, the graphic background and the type of mahjong pieces can be selected in the options menu. The game also features a story mode in which the player takes on the role of a mahjong player who arrives in a city and wants to become the mahjong champion.
