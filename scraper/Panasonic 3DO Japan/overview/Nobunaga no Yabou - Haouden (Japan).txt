Nobunaga no Yabou is a military-historical strategy game. You are transported to medieval Japan in the 16th century, a country torn by warlords fighting over the provinces. Play as Oda Nobunaga, one of Japan's most famous samurai, who dedicated his life to unifying this eastern nation. Three scenarios are available: 1551 - Nobunaga's rise, 1568 - the campaign against Kyoto, and 1582 - Nobunaga's death.
