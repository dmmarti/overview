Emit is a visual novel, divided into three parts, which has been designed to help Japanese people study English. The game can be played in English or Japanese. There are two game modes: Story mode and English game mode. The story mode is the main game divided into chapters where players can follow the storyline and do a number of exercises after each chapter. In the story mode, players can interrupt the game at any time to look up a term they did not understand or to change the language.
