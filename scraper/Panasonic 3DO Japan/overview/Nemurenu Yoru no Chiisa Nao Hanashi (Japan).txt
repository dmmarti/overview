Nemurenu Yoru no Chiisana Ohanashi (translated as “Little Stories for Sleepless Nights”) is a child-friendly adventure game inspired by the original animation of the same name. The story centers on Neko-kun, a cat living on a picturesque island alongside animal and human companions. The story unfolds as Neko-kun encounters a small star that has fallen to Earth and seeks to help it recover from its injuries.
