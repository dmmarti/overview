Once again these fiendish aliens from the Planet ODD are on the
rampage attacking your home planet. You must fight to the death
to protect your home.

There are 9 levels of difficulty which you select by inputting
the level you require.

You may now select 1 or 2 player game.
The Control Keys are as follows:
Fire            - SPACE
Left            - CAPS SHIFT
Right           - Z
Abandon Game    - A
Stop Game       - S Press any key to continue game.
Points may be scored for destroying each Galaxian.
Bottom 3 rows = 30 Points.
      4th row = 40 Points
      5th row = 50 Points
      Top row = 60 Points
Swooping Galaxians score double points!
This program works with the Kempston and Sinclair Joysticks.

GOOD LUCK
