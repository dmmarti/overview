The point and shoot gunner is a sequel to  Gunblade NY, and was released in 1998. The cabinet has two machine guns designed for the player to use whilst playing through the game. The game is based in the year 2025 in Los Angeles where players of the game can drive virtual hoverbikes attacking the enemy (futuristic attacking androids), the other objective of the game is to avoid accidentally killing civilians. The player can select one of the five levels at the beginning of the game all of the levels ranging in difficulty for the more veteran players.
