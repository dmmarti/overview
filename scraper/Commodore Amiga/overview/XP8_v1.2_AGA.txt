 A vertically scrolling shoot 'em up featuring raytraced graphics with lots of reflections. It comes on 4 disks AGA version or 3 disks OCS/ECS version and is hard-drive installable, although feasible from floppy.   8 different weapons are on offer, which can be powered up to several stages, and are collected from tokens, sometimes in hidden sections. A simultaneous 2-player mode is offered, which makes getting through the 5 levels with passwords for each one a little easier. The introduction takes the form of a few static screens.
