 Midwinter is a 1st Person strategy/role playing game with some first person shooting, and a unique story line. Attempt to defeat General Masters by blowing up his headquarters but first get past a multitude of patrols,recruiting new characters as you go,defeating cunning terrain,sabotaging enemy installations and a whole host of other options.  Controlling up to 32 characters, you must defend an immense playing area of over 160,000 square miles. You can ski, hang-glide, snow-buggy or travel by cable car across glacial landscape, shooting, sniping and sabotaging the intruders as you go.  Utilising a fractal generated landscape Midwinter submerged the player in a landscape of snowy peaks and desolate plains.
