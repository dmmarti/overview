 European answer to American Artworx Strip Poker, this game introduces one girl in MSX1 original version and two girls with different names and slightly different looks in other versions Amiga, Atari ST, DOS versus MSX2.  The playmate welcomes you in her apartment and proposes a card game. The game is a poker, and when player loses another $100, some part of her dress will be removed. The goal is to keep your clothes on and to make a girl completely naked.  The graphics in original MSX1 game is different, but other versions tries to keep conceptual design of original.
