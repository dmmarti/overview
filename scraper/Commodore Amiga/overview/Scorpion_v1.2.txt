Scorpion (also known as Fly Fighter) is 2D side-scrolling platform game. The player controls warrior named Scorpion, who must reclaim the princess of Scorpia. He must go left, avoid obstacles (like grave-holes) and fight against various aliens, skeletons, birds, slugs, robots, pirates, and even Daleks-like creatures (from "Doctor Who"). At the end of the each level Scorpion must defeat big boss. He collects various power-ups (various weapon upgrades and higher jumps). The game has 5 levels (each with own climate, like a cemetery of winter level) and over 100 types of enemies.
