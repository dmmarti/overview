The Master of Darkness has taken the possession of the Diamond Goodness splitting it into a thousand pieces and hiding them in his underground caves. Fear and terror are spreading since Diamond of Goodness doesn’t emit it’s positive energy anymore. As the best commander of the Federation, you are charged with the task of collecting the pieces of the diamond.

“Future Shock” is composed of three different levels which have to be flown through. Each level consist of several screens (horizontal and vertical). Collect in each setting up to nine diamonds eliminating as many adversaries as possible. As soon as you get all the diamonds, you automatically move onto the next level.
