 This is the first part of a computer trilogy of Germanyﾴs most popular and successful PandP-RPG Das Schwarze Auge.  You can create a party of six characters to explore a lovely crafted world called Aventurien. Each player is represented by a very detailed skill and attribute system, which is as complex as the well known ADandD game system.  In the first part of the trilogy, it is your task to stop Orkish hordes from plundering human settlements in the borderlands of Aventurien. Therefore youﾴll have to recover a mysterious sword forged by cyclops, called Die Schicksalsklinge =Blade of Destiny. Little is known about this sword and it will take a while to discover it and its secrets.
