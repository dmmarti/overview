 Van Helsing is summoned to the Bistritz village to kill Dracula and his brides.  Brides of Dracula is a two-player split-screen game with  gameplay similar to Spy vs. Spy. Each player human or CPU controls either the vampire hunter Van Helsing or Count Dracula.  As Dracula, the objective is to create 13 brides by finding women, biting and leading them back to the crypt, where 13 coffins are lined up waiting for their recipients. As Van Helsing, the goal is to find 13 tools to kill Dracula, bring them back to Helsing's home and store them in the cupboard.  The collected objects and women are displayed at the bottom of the character window, along with the health bar.  Both characters can be killed by certain hazardous environments and NPCs. Some of these dangers affect only one of the characters ghouls only attack Van Helsing, while priests and villagers only harm Dracula.  Each player starts with 3 lives. The game takes place in the church cemetery and the adjacent village.
