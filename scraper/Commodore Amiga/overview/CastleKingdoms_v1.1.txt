 A long time ago there was a old wizard who created a talisman that contained five gem stones. The talisman brought prosper to the land and it's inhabitants and all combined it gave much power. Then the dark lord tricked the wizard with his evil and stole the talisman. He used it to summon hordes of evil creatures. The gems were hidden in five castles and now it's your quest to recover them and save the world.  Castle Kingdoms is a isometric action adventure. You control a party of five adventures knight, princess, warrior, elf and wizard and must fight your way through five castles of evil. You control one character at a time and the other will follow you. While playing you will find objects that make the fight a little easier, such as health and shield potions and scrolls of different sorts.
