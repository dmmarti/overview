 An interrim between Alien Breed and it's sequel. Gameplay is the same as its predecessor - each level is a maze to be navigated through, while fighting off horders of aliens fun. The special edition included 12 new levels and a bunch of new features. This release included the dark missions for the first time, where only the glowing blue eyes of the aliens were visible. Another important feature added was the ability to jump to a level by means of a code.
