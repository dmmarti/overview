 Scenario Disk for the modern naval battle simulator based on Larry Bond's popular Harpoon board-game from 1980. Brings 16 new naval scenarios for use with Harpoon, 3 main allignments of allies: East vs. West, West vs. Arab, Arabs vs. Israelis, Countries included: Egypt, France, Greece, Iraq, Israel, Italy, Libya, Syria, Turkey, US, USSR. Over 60 new platforms, including hydrofoils, helicopter cruisers, attack helicopters, German-type 209 Submarines, and Mirage Fighter Bombers.
