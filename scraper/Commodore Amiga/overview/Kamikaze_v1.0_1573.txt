Kamikaze is 2D vertical scrolling shoot'em up, very similar to Flying Shark. The player is pilot of fighter, he has 6 lives. His mission is to destroy enemy forces. He flies forward over military bases and shoots to all enemies - other planes, tanks, trucks etc. His plane has cannon with unlimited ammo and several bombs (4 on start) to clean the screen from enemies. Some destroyed enemies drop power-ups. Sometimes he fights against bigger enemy. Game has coop multi-player mode to 2 players.
