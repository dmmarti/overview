Dungeons of Avalon is a classic dungeon-crawler, an RPG with the grid-based movement and turn-based movement, created by a group of developers - Zeret - for Amiga computers. It was originally released on coverdisk to the German magazine Amiga Fun (1991), later the Amiga Mania (1992). Inspired by games like Dungeon Master, Eye of the Beholder (from which developers replicate the graphics of the dungeon) and Wizardry, Avalon does not essentially nothing new, with too easy puzzles and fights, but deliver the very good level design of each dungeon...
