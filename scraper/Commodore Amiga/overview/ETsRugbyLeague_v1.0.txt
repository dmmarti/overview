E.T.'s Rugby League is a rugby league game developed by Denton Designs and published by Sega Ozisoft for the Amiga, Commodore 64 and PC in 1992. It is a conversion of World Class Rugby from a rugby union game to a rugby league game. It was developed specifically for the Australian market, featuring the endorsement of famed Cronulla Sharks centre Andrew "E.T." Ettingshausen and the official licenses of the NSWRL and ARL.
