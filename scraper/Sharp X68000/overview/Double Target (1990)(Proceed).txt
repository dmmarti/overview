After the dissolution of the Soviet Union in former Warsaw Pact countries many different gangs wanted to take control of as much territory as possible. However those who came later were not strong enough, soon mysterious bombing presents and leaden diseases appeared. Those who survived did not have money or did not want to involve them self into that so they decided to use mercenaries...  Player is one of those mercenaries. By choosing one of available classes (commando, saboteur or sniper) you enter Polish cities, woods, factories and work for local mafia.
