In 2048, a Strider named Hiryu travels the world in search of the mysterious Meio, who reigns on Earth from his lunar base. The player controls Hiryu, armed with his Falchion saber, through five levels ranging from the Soviet Union to the pristine forest. Hiryu has the particularity of being able to hang on the walls thanks to a metal hook, and can also be helped by two robotic animals (a hawk and a tiger) called Options.
