This edition of DOR is a collection of three short stories, taken from two different  DOR games:  From the  first DOR: Kanako's Depression: You are visited by a beautiful vampire who wants only one thing: To suck your blood. In return, she will be your servant for one day.  The Telephone Scandal: You call into a telephone dating service. You can choose to talk to a young schoolgirl or an older working woman. From  DOR 2: Armored Fighter Gyleban: Parody of good-versus-evil anime, such as Macross.  Each story can be finished in approximately ten minutes. DOR features depictions of sex, and is for adults only.
