Sonic Drift is a Sonic the Hedgehog racing game. The evil Dr. Robotnik has stolen the chaos emeralds and you have to win them back in a racing tournament. In Sonic Drift, you can play as Sonic, Tails, Amy, or Robotnik. Sonic Drift can also be found on Sonic Adventure DX and Sega Mega Collection Plus. There are six levels in each game and three different  tournaments, totaling 18 levels.
