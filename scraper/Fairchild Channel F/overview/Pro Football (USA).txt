This is a 2 player American Football game played on a horizontal football field. Players take control of a 6 player team and play both offense and defense against an opposing player's team with the objective to outscore their opponent. There are 8 offensive and 8 defensive plays for a total of 16 different plays to call.  On offense, the player can either pass the ball or run using the quarterback to try to score a touchdown. Also available is the option to kick a field goal to score points. On defense, players can have linebackers blitz or try to defend the pass from being completed.  The game contains four different game speeds which are slowest, slow, fast, and fastest.
