Detective is a game of deduction and tactics. The object is to establish, by means of elimination and deduction, the identity of the murderer of Lucky Duke. Your task is to find out who committed the crime, the weapon used, the place of the crime and the killer's motive. Each player plays one of the characters in the game. The computer will tell you which of the nine characters you are to play, and the murderer cannot be one of the players.</Notes
