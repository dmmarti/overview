Canyon Climber is a 1982 computer game designed by Steve Bjork and James Garon for the TRS-80 Color Computer and published by Datasoft. Canyon Climber is a three-screen platform game in the vein of Donkey Kong, but with an American Southwest theme. The game was ported to the Atari 8-bit family by Tim Ferris, the Apple II by Brian Mountford, and PC-6001. The box art is by Scott Ross.</Notes
