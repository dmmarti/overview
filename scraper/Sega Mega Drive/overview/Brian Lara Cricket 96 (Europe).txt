Brian Lara Cricket '96 is an updated version of Brian Lara Cricket on the SEGA Genesis. It is basically the same game, with updated sprites and roster.

The game features international matches, English county matches (with 18 teams) and 6 classic historical matches (from 1960 to 1994). Also available are the World Cup competition (with 12 national teams) and a Test Series. The game also features a team editor, battery back-up save and one to four multiplayer mode.
