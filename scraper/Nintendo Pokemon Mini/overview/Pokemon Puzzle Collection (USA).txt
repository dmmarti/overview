Pokémon Puzzle Collection, as the title suggests, is a collection of Pokémon related puzzles. In Motion Puzzle, you must choose two tiles to switch places in order to form a Pokémon's image. In Shadow Puzzle, you put shapes together to form the relative silhouette of a Pokémon. in Rescue Mission, you must free a Pokémon trapped by blocks by moving the blocks out of their way and off of the hole that they must escape through. After completing 30 puzzles, a fourth bonus game is unlocked, called Power On. In this game, you must light up a light bulb by creating a circuit between the light bulb and Pikachu.
