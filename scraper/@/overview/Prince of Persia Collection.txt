Prince of Persia video games are celebrated for their fluid animation, precise platforming, and cinematic presentation. Players navigate dangerous environments filled with traps, puzzles, and enemies.

The series emphasises timing, movement, and spatial awareness, with combat blended into traversal and exploration. Acrobatic movement and environmental storytelling create a sense of elegance and tension.

Prince of Persia is widely regarded as a milestone in action-adventure design.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
