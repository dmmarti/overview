Video games based on Golf translate the calm precision and strategic depth of the sport into interactive experiences that appeal to a wide range of players. These games span realistic simulations and lighthearted arcade-style interpretations, offering both authenticity and accessibility. Players must carefully manage swing power, accuracy, club selection, and environmental factors such as wind, terrain, and hazards.

Simulation-focused golf games emphasize realism, featuring detailed courses, physics-based ball movement, and career or tournament modes where players compete across full seasons. Arcade-style golf games simplify controls and add exaggerated shots, creative characters, and playful mechanics, making them ideal for quick and social play. Many titles also include multiplayer modes, allowing friends to compete locally or cooperatively.

Golf games are known for their relaxing pace, immersive sound design, and rewarding mastery. Whether carefully lining up a perfect putt or driving the ball hundreds of metres down the fairway, these games capture both the challenge and enjoyment of the sport in digital form.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
