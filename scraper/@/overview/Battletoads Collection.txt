Video games based on Battletoads are known for their intense action, over-the-top humour, and punishing difficulty. Originally developed by Rare, the series follows the adventures of the anthropomorphic toads Rash, Zitz, and Pimple as they battle bizarre enemies across a variety of chaotic environments. Battletoads games quickly earned a reputation for challenging gameplay that tests reflexes, timing, and memorisation.
