Road Rash video games combine motorcycle racing with combat, creating aggressive and chaotic gameplay. Players race at high speeds while fighting rivals using punches and weapons.

Risk-taking, timing, and track knowledge are key to success. Crashes are frequent and punishing, adding tension and excitement.

Road Rash is remembered for its rebellious tone and unique blend of racing and action.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
