Joust video games are classic arcade action titles with unique physics-based gameplay. Players control a flying knight mounted on an ostrich or stork, battling enemy knights in mid-air combat.

Success depends on mastering flight height and momentum, as higher positioning determines victory in collisions. The simple controls hide surprising depth, making matches intense and competitive. Cooperative and competitive multiplayer options add to the game’s replayability.

Joust’s originality, fast-paced action, and memorable design have made it a standout arcade classic.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
