Video games starring Donkey Kong are foundational to video game history, introducing some of the earliest and most influential platforming mechanics. Originally debuting in arcades, the series follows the adventures of Donkey Kong and other characters through a variety of action-packed challenges.

Gameplay often focuses on precise jumping, obstacle avoidance, and timing-based challenges, later evolving into expansive platform adventures. Known for its colourful visuals, memorable music, and inventive level design, Donkey Kong has remained relevant across generations.
