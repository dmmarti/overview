Video games based on Hockey bring the speed, physicality, and excitement of ice hockey into interactive form. These games range from realistic simulations that focus on strategy, positioning, and player control to arcade-style titles that emphasise fast action and exaggerated checks and shots.

Players typically manage skating, passing, shooting, and defending while reacting quickly to the rapid pace of play. Many hockey games include season modes, tournaments, and multiplayer options, allowing players to compete against friends or AI opponents. Realistic sound effects, crowd noise, and commentary often enhance immersion.

Whether aiming for authenticity or pure fun, hockey video games capture the intensity and energy of the sport, making them appealing to both fans and casual players.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
