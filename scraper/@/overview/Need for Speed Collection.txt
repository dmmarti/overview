Need for Speed video games focus on high-speed racing, car customisation, and adrenaline-fuelled competition. Players race exotic cars through urban streets, highways, and scenic routes.

The series balances arcade-style handling with progression systems that reward upgrades and tuning. Police chases and illegal street racing themes add excitement and tension.

Need for Speed is known for its stylish presentation, licensed cars, and emphasis on speed and freedom.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
