The Simpsons video games bring the humour, characters, and exaggerated world of the long-running animated series into interactive form. These games often feature beat ’em up action, platforming, or adventure gameplay, allowing players to control members of the Simpson family through colourful versions of Springfield. Faithful visual styles, voice clips, and comedic scenarios capture the show’s tone. Whether fighting quirky enemies or exploring familiar locations, Simpsons games emphasise fun, accessibility, and fan service.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
