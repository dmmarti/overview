OutRun video games are classic arcade racing titles focused on speed, atmosphere, and freedom. Players race along branching scenic routes, choosing paths that affect difficulty and scenery.

Rather than strict competition, OutRun emphasizes smooth driving, time management, and visual style. Iconic music and colourful landscapes create a relaxed yet thrilling experience.

OutRun remains a timeless example of arcade racing done with style and personality.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
