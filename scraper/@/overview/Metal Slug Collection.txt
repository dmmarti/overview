Metal Slug video games are run-and-gun action titles celebrated for their fast-paced gameplay, hand-drawn animation, and explosive combat. Players battle waves of enemies using a wide arsenal of weapons and vehicles.

The series is known for its humour, expressive visuals, and cooperative gameplay. Tight controls and constant action demand quick reflexes, while branching paths encourage replayability.

Metal Slug stands out for its visual craftsmanship and arcade intensity, remaining a fan favourite for action enthusiasts.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
