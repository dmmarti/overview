Mario Party video games focus on multiplayer fun, combining board-game mechanics with fast-paced mini-games. Players compete to collect stars while navigating dynamic boards filled with surprises, hazards, and strategic decisions.

The mini-games span a wide variety of challenges, testing reflexes, memory, teamwork, and timing. Random events and item use keep matches unpredictable, ensuring no two sessions play the same way.

Mario Party is designed for group play, emphasizing laughter, rivalry, and friendly chaos. Its colourful presentation and simple controls make it accessible, while its competitive nature keeps players engaged.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
