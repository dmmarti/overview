Raiden video games are vertical scrolling shooters known for intense action and military-themed visuals. Players pilot advanced aircraft against relentless enemy forces.

Power-ups enhance weapons and defence, while dense bullet patterns test reflexes and positioning. The series is praised for clarity, challenge, and arcade pacing.

Raiden remains a staple of classic shooter design.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
