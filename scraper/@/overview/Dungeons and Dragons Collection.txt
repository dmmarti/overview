Dungeons & Dragons video games translate the rich fantasy worlds and role-playing depth of the tabletop game into digital adventures. These titles often focus on character creation, exploration, tactical combat, and narrative-driven quests set in expansive fantasy settings.

Players can choose from various races and classes, developing skills and abilities while facing monsters, traps, and moral choices. Many games emphasize teamwork, strategy, and storytelling, staying true to the spirit of the original tabletop experience. Across platforms and decades, Dungeons & Dragons games offer deep, immersive role-playing adventures.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
