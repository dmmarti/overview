Video games based on **Batman** immerse players in the dark, crime-filled streets of Gotham City, allowing them to step into the role of the Dark Knight himself. Drawing inspiration from comic books, television shows, and blockbuster films, these games combine action, stealth, and detective work to capture the essence of one of pop culture’s most iconic superheroes.

Batman games often feature a mix of combat, exploration, and puzzle-solving. Players can patrol Gotham’s rooftops, engage in hand-to-hand battles with notorious villains like the Joker, Penguin, and Riddler, and use an array of gadgets and vehicles, including the Batmobile and Batarangs, to overcome obstacles. Many titles also incorporate detective mechanics, challenging players to investigate crime scenes, gather clues, and solve complex mysteries.

Visually, Batman games are known for their moody, atmospheric settings, detailed environments, and dramatic lighting that reflect the noir tone of the franchise. Story-driven entries offer cinematic narratives and memorable character interactions, while arcade-style or action-focused adaptations emphasize fast-paced combat and gadget-based gameplay. Across consoles, computers, and handhelds, Batman games have evolved in style and scope but consistently deliver the thrill of being a superhero in a world where danger lurks around every corner.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
