Video games in the Shinobi series focus on fast-paced ninja action, combining precise platforming with stylish combat. Players typically take on the role of a skilled ninja battling criminal organisations and supernatural enemies using shurikens, swords, and special abilities. Timing, reflexes, and mastery of movement are essential, as levels demand careful jumps, wall climbs, and rapid attacks. Shinobi games are known for their challenging difficulty, memorable soundtracks, and distinctive Japanese-inspired settings. Over the years, the series has evolved visually while maintaining its emphasis on skill-based gameplay and intense action.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
