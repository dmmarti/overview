Mario Kart video games blend fast-paced racing with chaotic fun, transforming traditional kart racing into a party-like experience. Players race as iconic characters across imaginative tracks filled with shortcuts, hazards, and interactive elements.

The defining feature of Mario Kart is its item system, allowing racers to use power-ups such as shells, boosts, and traps to gain advantages or disrupt opponents. This creates unpredictable races where skill and strategy mix with luck, keeping matches exciting until the finish line.

Accessible controls make Mario Kart easy to pick up, while advanced techniques reward mastery. Multiplayer modes are a core highlight, making the series a favourite for social and competitive play alike.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
