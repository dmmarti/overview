Video games starring Mario are among the most influential and beloved titles in gaming history. Beginning as simple arcade platformers, the series evolved into expansive adventures that defined and refined the platforming genre. Players guide Mario through imaginative worlds filled with enemies, power-ups, secrets, and memorable characters, relying on precise jumping, timing, and exploration.

The Mario series is known for its creative level design, tight controls, and constant innovation. From side-scrolling stages to fully realised 3D worlds, each game introduces new mechanics while maintaining a welcoming and fun tone. Power-ups such as mushrooms, fire flowers, and suits dramatically change gameplay, encouraging experimentation and replayability.

Bright visuals, iconic music, and universal appeal have made Mario a symbol of video games themselves. The franchise balances accessibility for newcomers with depth for experienced players, ensuring its popularity across generations.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
