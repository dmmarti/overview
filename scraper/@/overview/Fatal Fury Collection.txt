Video games in the Fatal Fury series are classic one-on-one fighting games that helped establish SNK as a major force in the genre. Set in a gritty, character-driven world, the series focuses on martial arts combat, rivalries, and personal stories.

Fatal Fury introduced several innovative mechanics, including multi-plane fighting, allowing characters to move between foreground and background lanes. Combat emphasizes timing, spacing, and mastering special moves, making each match feel tactical and intense.
