Tomb Raider video games are landmark action-adventure titles starring the intrepid explorer Lara Croft. Players explore ancient ruins, solve environmental puzzles, and navigate dangerous landscapes filled with traps, enemies, and hidden treasures. The series is defined by its mix of platforming, exploration, and combat.

Gameplay emphasizes agility, precision, and strategic use of weapons and tools. Players often engage in acrobatic feats such as climbing, jumping, and swinging, while facing adversaries ranging from wildlife to human enemies. Storylines frequently weave archaeological discovery with mystery and danger, creating a cinematic experience enhanced by detailed environments, atmospheric music, and immersive visuals.
