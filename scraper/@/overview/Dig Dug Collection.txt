Video games in the Dig Dug series blend action and strategy with a unique underground setting. Players dig tunnels through the earth, avoiding and defeating enemies by inflating them or crushing them with falling rocks. The maze-like environments encourage clever planning and quick thinking.

Dig Dug is remembered for its colourful characters, simple controls, and inventive mechanics. As enemies can move through walls, players must constantly adapt, using the terrain to their advantage. The gradual increase in difficulty keeps gameplay engaging and rewarding. Its originality and charm have made Dig Dug a timeless favourite across multiple generations.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
