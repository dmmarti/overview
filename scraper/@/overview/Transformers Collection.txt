Transformers video games allow players to control robots that can transform into vehicles, planes, or other machinery, bringing dynamic combat and exploration to life. Players often take on the roles of Autobots or Decepticons, engaging in battles across urban, industrial, and alien environments.

Gameplay varies from action-adventure and shooters to fighting and racing, often emphasizing transformation mechanics that allow players to adapt between combat and movement modes. Story-driven campaigns explore the ongoing struggle between heroic Autobots and evil Decepticons, incorporating cinematic storytelling, mission objectives, and challenging enemies.

Transformers video games capture the thrill of piloting giant robots, strategic transformation, and the epic battles that define the franchise. Fans enjoy the blend of fast-paced action, vehicle mechanics, and iconic characters brought to interactive life.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
