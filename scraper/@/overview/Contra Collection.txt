Video games in the Contra series are renowned for their high-intensity run-and-gun action and legendary difficulty. Players battle through enemy-infested environments using powerful weapons, quick reflexes, and precise movement.

The series emphasizes nonstop action, cooperative gameplay, and memorable boss fights. Weapon power-ups dramatically change combat style, while fast scrolling levels keep pressure constant. Contra is celebrated for its tight controls, iconic music, and demanding challenge.

Its influence on action games remains strong decades after its debut.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
