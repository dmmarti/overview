Video games in the Double Dragon series helped define the beat ’em up genre with cooperative action and street-fighting gameplay. Players control martial artists battling through urban environments filled with gangs, weapons, and boss encounters.

The series emphasizes hand-to-hand combat, combos, and cooperative play, allowing two players to fight side by side. Double Dragon is known for its straightforward mechanics, escalating difficulty, and memorable enemy designs. Its success paved the way for countless beat ’em up games, cementing its place as a genre-defining classic.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
