The King of Fighters video games are team-based fighting games that bring together characters from multiple SNK franchises. Players select teams of fighters and battle opponents in sequential one-on-one matches.

The series is known for its deep mechanics, precise controls, and competitive focus. Strategy extends beyond individual fights, as team order and resource management play key roles. Rich character backstories and evolving storylines add narrative depth.

King of Fighters remains a cornerstone of competitive fighting games worldwide.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
