Video games based on **Basketball** bring the fast-paced excitement, skill, and strategy of the court to players of all ages. From realistic simulations to arcade-style action, these games capture the intensity of the sport, allowing players to control teams, star athletes, and even manage entire seasons.

Basketball games typically feature dribbling, passing, shooting, and defensive mechanics that replicate the flow of a real match. Many titles include career and franchise modes, where players can draft teams, develop skills, and compete for championships over multiple seasons. Arcade-style versions focus on high-energy gameplay, often with exaggerated dunks, flashy moves, and power-ups, delivering quick, fun, and accessible action without sacrificing excitement.

Over the years, basketball video games have evolved across various platforms, offering realistic graphics, immersive commentary, and detailed stadium environments. Players can experience one-on-one street games, full league matches, or tournament-style competitions, often with multiplayer options that add social and competitive layers. Whether executing a perfect alley-oop, strategizing plays, or leading a team to victory, basketball games combine skill, strategy, and entertainment in a way that mirrors the thrill of the sport itself.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
