Video games based on Dragon Ball bring the high-energy action, humour, and epic battles of the iconic anime and manga series into interactive form. These games allow players to step into the roles of legendary characters such as Goku, Vegeta, and many others, recreating famous story arcs and intense rivalries from the series.

Dragon Ball games often focus on fast-paced combat, featuring powerful melee attacks, energy blasts, and dramatic special moves. Many titles emphasise aerial battles, large arenas, and destructible environments, capturing the scale and spectacle of the anime. Some entries include role-playing elements, allowing players to level up characters, unlock abilities, and explore open areas between battles.

With vibrant visuals, dynamic animations, and energetic soundtracks, Dragon Ball games successfully convey the excitement and over-the-top nature of the franchise. Across different platforms and generations, these games have appeared in various styles, from traditional fighting games to action-adventure experiences, appealing to both long-time fans and new players alike.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
