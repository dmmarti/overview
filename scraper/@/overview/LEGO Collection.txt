LEGO video games combine humour, creativity, and accessible gameplay across a wide range of licensed and original worlds. These games often adapt popular films and franchises into lighthearted action-adventure experiences.

Gameplay focuses on exploration, puzzle-solving, and cooperative play, with players breaking objects, collecting studs, and unlocking characters. The visual style mimics real LEGO bricks, adding charm and consistency across titles.

LEGO games are especially known for their family-friendly design, cooperative fun, and playful reinterpretation of familiar stories.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
