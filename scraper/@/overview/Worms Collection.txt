Worms video games are turn-based strategy titles featuring cartoonish armies of worms battling across destructible landscapes. Players plan attacks using a variety of weapons, including bazookas, grenades, and the iconic sheep, aiming to outsmart opponents while navigating environmental hazards.

The games emphasize strategy, timing, and creativity, as well as humour and unpredictability. Multiplayer modes are central, allowing friends to compete locally or online, making Worms both tactical and socially engaging.

Worms combines challenging strategy, inventive weaponry, and comedic presentation for enduring appeal.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
