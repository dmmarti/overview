Video games based on **The Addams Family** bring the franchise’s signature mix of dark humour, spooky atmosphere, and quirky charm into interactive form. Inspired by the classic characters created by Charles Addams and their many television and film appearances, these games invite players into a world where the strange is normal and the creepy is playful rather than frightening.

Most Addams Family games focus on action, platforming, and adventure gameplay, often set within the family’s iconic mansion and its surrounding environments. Players explore secret rooms, hidden passages, graveyards, and dungeons while avoiding traps, battling unusual enemies, and solving light puzzles. Different family members may be featured, each reflecting the offbeat personality and tone that defines the series. Exploration and curiosity are commonly rewarded, encouraging players to search every corner for secrets and power-ups.

What makes these games stand out is their distinctive mood. Instead of traditional horror, they embrace comedy and personality, pairing eerie visuals with humorous animations and catchy, memorable music. Across a wide range of systems and generations, Addams Family video games offer nostalgic fun and creative design, capturing the timeless appeal of one of pop culture’s most delightfully unconventional families.

This collection contains games from Arcades, Computers, Consoles and Handhelds.
