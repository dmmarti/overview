PD Ultraman Link  is a 1996 puzzle game for the Sega Saturn by SIMS and Bandai tying into the Ultraman franchise. It was only released in Japan.    It appears to be a falling-blocks puzzle game in which you connect three or more pieces of the same color that connect in predefined ways.    Super deformed Ultraman and pals themed puzzler in which you must align capsules to make a chain. Some very satisfying combos possible in a game that slowly gets its hooks into you as you get used to the unusual, yet refreshing, style.
