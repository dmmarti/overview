Oshaberi Parodius, known on the Sega Saturn and PlayStation as Jikky? Oshaberi Parodius ~forever with me~, is the fourth game in a series of parody shooters produced by Konami. The gameplay is stylistically very similar to the Gradius series, but the graphics and music are intentionally absurd.
