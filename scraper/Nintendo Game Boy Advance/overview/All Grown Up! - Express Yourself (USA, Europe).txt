Join Nickelodeon's Rugrats on another great adventure with your Game Boy Advance in Rugrats: All Grown Up: Express Yourself. Based on the hit Nickelodeon All Grown Up TV series, players will help Tommy, Angelica, and the rest of the Rugrats gang as they leave behind childhood and enter the pre-teen world of middle school. Braces, retainers, and pimples are now the challenges of the day and the Rugrats gang will have to pull together to make it through more milestones.
