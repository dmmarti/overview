The island of Mata Nui holds a dangerous path of evil. In order to defeat it, you must control two heroes in order to solve the puzzles and defeat the enemy characters along the way.

Based on the Lego toy series, Bionicle, your task is to defeat the Bahrag. The two characters you control both have their advantages, and you must swap between the two during play in order to get through each of the 6 worlds.
