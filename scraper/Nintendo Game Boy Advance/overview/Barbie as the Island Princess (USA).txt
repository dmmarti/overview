Barbie as The Island Princess follows a "party-style" storyline. You'll play as Rosella, the shipwrecked princess, who is raised by a "family" of animal friends and rescued by Prince Antonio from her tropical island. Through mini-games you will venture from Rosella's island home to the Prince's coastal castle for more adventure. Features: •Interact with Barbie as Rosella, Prince Antonio, Princess Luciana, Tika, Sagi, Azul or Tallulah •Visit the Deserted Island, Royal Ship, Harbor Village, Apollonian Castle and the Greenhouse •28 mini games including flower collecting, dress designing, star gazing, dancing and item collecting •Play by yourself or in co-op and competitive multiplayer •Includes footage and songs from the movie •Unlock special characters, dresses and additional movie footage
