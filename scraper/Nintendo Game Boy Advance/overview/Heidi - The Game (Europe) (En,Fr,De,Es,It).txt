Heidi: The Game: Have fun with your GameBoy into the wonderful world of Heidi.  Set in the beautiful mountains and in the metropolis of Frankfurt Heidi communicates with the animals he meets along the way simply whistling, but beware not all will be docile and friendly.  Explore the mountains and valleys and complete all missions.  Guaranteed fun with extra hidden missions  Immediate title to play, but an incredible longevity
