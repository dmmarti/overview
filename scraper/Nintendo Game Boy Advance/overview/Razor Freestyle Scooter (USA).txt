Razor Freestyle Scooter is the opportunity to take your scooter into the funbox! Control urban thrashers who cut through massive, radical environments. Perform tight tricks, like heel-flips, candybars, can-cans and supermen. Add to the time bar by pulling off phat air on insane ramps and half-pipes. Grind ledges, high-flying wires, barrels and benches. Collect wheels scattered throughout each level to unlock the coolest parks and characters!
