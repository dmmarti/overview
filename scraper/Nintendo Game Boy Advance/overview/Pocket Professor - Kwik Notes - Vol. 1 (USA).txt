Pocket Professor "Kwik Notes" is a cool school tool that provides quick access to basic math, algebra, geometry, English, computer basics, geography, American History, World History, U.S. Government and sciences like Chemistry and physics all on the Game Boy Advance. Automatically convert Celsius to Fahrenheit, review grammar and punctuation rules, and actually hear the proper pronunciations of many commonly used foreign words and phrases in the English language today, all at the touch of a button.
