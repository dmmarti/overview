Play with the Pros as kids on Nintendo Game Boy Advance! It’s great football action with the Backyard Kids and real pro players as kids. Little football fans will enjoy choosing from all 32 NFL teams or creating their own with custom team names and uniforms. Call the plays and direct all the action in single games or an entire season’s worth of hard-hitting action. It’s the chance of a lifetime to play with the pros right in your own backyard! Features: •Play with 10 pro players as kids including Donovan Mcnabb, Peyton Manning, Terrell Davis, Brett Farve, Ricky Williams, Jeff Garcia, Jevon Kearse, Jerry Rice, Michael Vick, and Rich Gannon •Play with the 30 "Backyard Kids" •Choose from all 32 NFL teams •Pick your field, call the plays, and direct all the action •Play head-to-head using a Game Link cable •Create, save, and trade custom players •Track player and team stats in "Season Play"
