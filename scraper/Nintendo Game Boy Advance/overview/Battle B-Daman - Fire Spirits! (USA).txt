Battle B-Daman 2 is the follow-up to Battle B-Daman, the game based on the popular animated show and action toys. Return to the life of Yamato Delgado as he faces a whole new group of opponents while playing B-DaMan, the toughest and craziest sport ever invented! Meet all-new characters as you journey to each B-DaMan court • Battle for fame, glory and goodness with new weapons and moves • Use the Cobalt Blade to compete in B-Daman tournaments and defeat the evil B-Daplayers of the Shadow Alliance
