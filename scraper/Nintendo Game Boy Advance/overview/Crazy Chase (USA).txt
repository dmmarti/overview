Strap on you turbo-charges clown shoes! It's up to you to help Kid Klown find four items that will help him reach the next stage before Dirty Joe's bomb detonates. The stages in Crazy Chase are strewn with products of peril like cars, thorns, spikes, cannons, sticky bushes, rolling logs and other obtrusive obstacles that must be overcome by your Galahad in greasepaint. Quick thinking, fast fingers and a sense of humor are essential of winning this Crazy Chase!
