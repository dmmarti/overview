Relp! Scooby-Doo and the gang have been beamed into cyberspace, and they are not alone. The crafty Phantom Virus is up to his old tricks, and he's got a band of dastardly villains at his disposal. Search for clues, fend off his evil henchmen, and maybe even unmask the Phantom Virus himself The game's 18 levels of Scooby action let you explore a creepy mine, avoid creepy mummies, and throw cream-filled pies to defeat the Phantom and his cronies. All the while, you'll play through environments and scenarios based on the Scooby-Doo movie's story line and artwork.
