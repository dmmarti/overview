Jackie Chan plays the role of Passepartout, the French valet of the inventor Phileas Fogg. When Fogg takes on a bet, against his nemesis Lord Kelvin that he can travel around the world in 80 days, Passepartout agrees to accompany him on the journey. Joined in Paris by a young lady named Monique the three face a variety of adventures and escapades as they try to make their way across the globe.
