Sam, Alex, and Clover are bests friends and Totally normal high school girls. They got to school, they giggle at boys, and more than anything else, they love to shop. But then, one day, they get recruited by WOOHP the "World Organization Of Human Protection" to be secret agents and become Totally Spies!. Play the 16 levels on five different story. Use the super cool gadgets to defeat the bad guys.
