When a mean-spirited hamster named Spat starts squabbles among the rest of the hamsters in Hamtaro: Ham-Ham Heartbreak, Hamtaro and Bijou set out to get friends to make up. As they mend fences, they get help from Harmony, an angelic hamster with a heart of gold. Along the way, they have to learn the secret hamster language known as Ham-Chat in order to solve problems and move on.
