Can you help the mixed up Mighty Beanz get themselves together? They've spilt apart and scrambled and it's up to you to match the right tops with the right bottoms. The more you match, the more Mighty Beanz points you get. Unlock rare and super pro Beanz and wager them in Battle Mode, and use special power-ups to send your opponent's Beanz spinning. For 1 or 2 players.
