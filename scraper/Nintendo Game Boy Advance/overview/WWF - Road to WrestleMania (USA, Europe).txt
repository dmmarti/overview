The best wrestlers of the WWE take each other on for the championship belt, under your control. Choose from tag team, one-on-one, cage matches, handicap, triple threat and fatal four way battles in 7 different venues.  Pay-Per-View mode has you competing for ratings. Choose the right venue to fight in and suceed in the fight to gain high ratings and unlock new venues. After every victory you also pick up special WWE items including CD's, toys and DVD's, collect them all to prove you are the true champ.
