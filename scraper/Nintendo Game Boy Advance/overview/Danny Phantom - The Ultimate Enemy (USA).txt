The future is in danger from Clockwork, a sinister ghost with the power to control time. He wants to turn Danny Fenton into the future's evil Dan Phantom by destroying everything and everyone he cares about. Danny will have to learn new powers and foil Clockwork's plans if he wants to save his friends and family from the most dangerous force imaginable, himself. Danny Phantom's first video game which contains all of the superhero's action and humor from the TV series allowing you to use Danny's incredible superpowers in an exciting ghost-catching adventure.
