If you crave extreme sports action, then join Otto, Twister, Reggie, and Sam as they test their skills in the skatepark. Rocket Power: Zero Gravity Zone lets you play as all four members from Team Rocket Power plus one unlockable character. Test your skills in a variety of gameplay modes, including free skate, career, and multiplayer. Special power-ups, which you can find throughout eight different environments, help you perform more tricks and enhance your character's skills.
