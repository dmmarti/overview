Game Boy Advance receives this arcade port of Ultimate Mortal Kombat 3.  Comes complete with single-player mode, over 20 fighters, link mode for up to 4 players and lots of extras to unlock.

Features the classic 2D, side-scrolling fighting that made the original Mortal Kombat so popular.  Fatalities, Brutalities and even Friendship moves have made the cross over into this port.
