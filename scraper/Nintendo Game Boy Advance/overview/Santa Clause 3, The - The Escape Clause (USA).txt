The Santa Clause 3: The Escape Clause is an action-adventure game with all the humor and merriment of Disney's hit holiday film! Santa must overcome treacherous falling icicles, evil snowmen, powerful gusts of wind and numerous more obstacles to complete his holiday quest. Also included are puzzle-based challenges in which the player must help Santa's elves build toys in time for Christmas. Santa also takes to the skies in his sleigh where he battles the elements to deliver gifts to good boys and girls (and coal to the naughty).
