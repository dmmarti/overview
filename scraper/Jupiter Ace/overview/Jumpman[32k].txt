You are Jumpman and your aim in this single screen platform game is to collect all the fruit before ringing a bell on the top left of the screen. You must collect all the fruits in a certain order with the next fruit to collect shown on the bottom of the screen. There are various creatures and obstacles that need avoiding like springs, skulls and gaps in the platforms and if you touch one then you lose one of three lives. To get to higher platforms you can take a lift but can lose a life if not entered or exited correctly.</Notes
