Ambushed by pirates, Reader Rabbit and Sam the Lion must race to the top of Mt. Cheesemore to recover the Dreamship before the Pirates do!  Can you help them?    Features:  Educational mini-games designed to teach basic Language Arts, Science, Problem Solving, and Math as well as Creative Exploration and Expression.  Multiple difficulty levels for each mini-game that can be changed at any time.  Full video cutscenes and voice acting throughout the game.  Simple controls using the Wii Remote.
