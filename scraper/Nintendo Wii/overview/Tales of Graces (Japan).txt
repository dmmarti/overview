Tales of Graces (Japanese: ???? ?? ?????, Hepburn: Teiruzu Obu Gureisesu) is a Japanese role-playing game released for the Wii on December 10, 2009 in Japan. The game is a core product of the Tales series and was developed by Namco Tales Studio. The game was ported to the PlayStation 3 under the title Tales of Graces f (???? ?? ????? ??, Teiruzu Obu Gureisesu Efu) and was released on December 2, 2010 in Japan. The PlayStation 3 version was localized for North America on March 13, 2012 and Europe on August 31, 2012.
