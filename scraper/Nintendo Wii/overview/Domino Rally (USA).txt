Ever wanted to see a domino help you out? Now you can with Domino Rally - part platformer, part racer, and all fun. Use careful domino paths to help people with their everyday problems. Use the Wii control scheme to precisely set, balance, create, and topple your very own domino paths. There are secret paths to find and explore, as well as 90 secret items, memories, and additional content to discover as you race through the courses. The humor will keep you laughing from start to finish and the unique visuals and one-of-a-kind gameplay will have you zipping and zooming with all kinds of tiles!
