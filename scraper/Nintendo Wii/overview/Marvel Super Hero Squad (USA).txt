Welcome to Super Hero City! Assemble your squad and fight your way to victory to save the citizens of Super Hero City from the evil schemes of the super villains in this multi-player adventure game inspired by the action-packed animated Marvel TV series.Adventure Mode:* One Super Sized story and 6 action-packed campaigns featuring Wolverine, Hulk, Thor, Iron Man, Silver Surfer and Falcon.* 2 Bonus unlockable campaigns featuring Spider-Man and Dr. Doom.* Race to find Infinity Fractals and gain new and unpredictable super powers.* Team up with a friend to electrocute enemies, wrap them in webs, and save Super Hero City.* Unlock characters, costumes, battle arenas, and collectibles.* Seize your adventure with up to 2 players. Battle Mode:* Go head-to-head with your friends and fight as your favorite Super Hero or Villain.* Choose from over 20 Marvel characters from the X-Men, Fantastic Four, and Avengers series and use their unique super powers to smash, bash, and zap your enemies.* Create your own combo attacks to takedown the competition with unique character powers.* Choose a dynamic battle arena and trigger environmental events as you fight.* Fight the fun fight with up to 4 players: all-in, 2-on-2, or 3-on-1.
