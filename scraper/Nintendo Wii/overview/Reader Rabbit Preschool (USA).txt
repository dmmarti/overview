Join Reader Rabbit and Sam the Lion as they help the colorful characters of Sparkalot restore the missing sparkles to the land and sky!    Features:  Educational mini-games designed to teach basic Preschool skills including Letters and Sounds, Matching Attributes, Shapes, and Colors, Creative Expression, and Counting.  Multiple difficulty levels for each miniigame that can be changed at any time.  Full video cutscenes and voice acting throughout the game  Simple controls using the Wii Remote.
