Get in a spin with great game…The Magic Roundabout is the direct videogame adaptation from the popular TV show for kids, recently CGI remaked.It's Friendship Day, a celebration invented by the waggish Zebedee. At this occasion, he promises Dougal a wonderful gift if he succeeds in helping all the other people in the village. So does begin Doogal's adventure…Features* Meet all the famous characters from the show: Dougal the grumpy dog, Florence the kind hearted girl, Zebedee the strange magician, Ermintrude the cow and Dylan the sleepy rabbit…* 16 different minigames, plus unlockable bonus missions to discover within the story.* Easy to use interface : Nintendo Wii remote giving you full control of your favourite characters. shake it to find hidden objects in jars and use it to conduct an orchestra with your every move.* Original story, keeping all the atmosphere and humor of the series, plus cinematic cut scene from the TV series
