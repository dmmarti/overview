By the year 3011, a warming planet has buried our modern world beneath the waves. The nations that survived and adapted have formed the Earth Federation, and aim to bring peace and progress back tot he human experience.     But the Chaos Empire seeks only world domination. As the pilot of the prototype super-sub "Metal Fin", your task is to penetrate the defenses of the Chaos Empire and defeat their leader, the evil Dr. Shark.     Ocean Commander brings modern style and technology to classic side-scrolling gameplay. Enhance your ship with 12 dazzling, upgradeable weapons, then battle your way through 19 intense missions using intuitive pointing controls!     Features:  19 intense missions   12 awesome weapons, each with mutiple upgrades   Classic side-scrolling gameplay combined with modern graphics   Intuitive pointing controls for immersive action   Campaign mode and Time Attack mode
