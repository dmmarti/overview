Experience a brand new way to play on Wii as Skylanders Spyro's Adventure transports real-world action figures into the game environment before your eyes!    Every copy of Skylanders Spyro's Adventure comes with a set of action figures and a special platform called the Portal of Power. Placing the figures on the surface of the Portal of Power causes them to appear in the game world for you to control ? turning your action figures into interaction figures in an instant!
