Lucha Libre AAA: Héroes del Ring is an action sports game based on Mexican sport of 'Lucha Libre' style wrestling. The first video game to cover Mexican wrestling, Lucha Libre AAA: Héroes del Ring features dozens of real wrestlers from Mexico's premiere wrestling federation. Each of these possesses the acrobatic, over-the-top action that Lucha Libre, and which inspired modern professional wrestling around the world. Additional game features include: a popularity based scoring system, the ability to create you own luchador, authentic Mexican wrestling venues and more.
