The world is under constant threat from supervillains, megalomaniacs, and all-around badguys. To keep the peace, you ll need courage, cunning, and stylish flats!     Totally Spies! Totally Party brings the fun of the hit series to the Wii in a zany 3-player party game. Chase villains across 10 colorful gameboards while competing in outrageous mini-games, setting traps, and deploying clever spy gadgets.     Along the way, you ll unlock hot new clothes for each spy. Wear them in-game, or dress for success in a glamorous catwalk beat-game. You ll need style and grace to rule the fashion world!    Features:  Compete in 30 outrageous mini-games   Traverse 10 game boards, with themes from the show  Unlock tons of new clothes for each spy  Rock the catwalk by dressing up and strutting with confidence
