Top Spin returns for a fourth time, and the tennis franchise has brought a very special guest. Superstar Andre Agassi makes his first video game appearance in a decade in Top Spin 4! Choose from the deepest Top Spin roster yet and take the court with the best in the world. Dig in for grueling battles designed to deliver the same intensity of an epic boxing match. Each point is packed with remarkable detail, from the spin on the ball to the signature actions of your favorite players. Whether you're playing online or off, you can improve by earning experience points or hiring coaches who help you develop specific skills.
