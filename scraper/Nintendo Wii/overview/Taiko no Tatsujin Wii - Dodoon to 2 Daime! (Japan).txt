Taiko no Tatsujin Wii: Do-Don to 2 Daime! is a Music/Rhythm game published by Bandai Namco Games released on November 19, 2009 for the Nintendo Wii.nnnnThis is the second Taiko WII game out of five. Enjoy this game songs with costumes and story mode. You might know a couple of songs like “I Want(IDOMASTER)” and the Dragon Ball Z theme song. Enjoy the only Taiko game that has boss fights for the WII.
