Think! Logic Trainer is an original brain training game that can help improve many cognitive abilities through play. It helps enhance language, maths, logic skills and spatial reasoning. All activities are presented on fun, imaginative and creative way. Think! Logic Trainer, a fun educational game for the whole family. It encourages you to think outside the box and help you keep your brain fit.   There are over 80000 stimulating different exercises to challenge your well toned mind. All exercises presented encourage the coordination of the right and left hemisphere of your brain. Training and stimulation of these area can help improve creativity, logical thinking and develops imagination.     Think! Logic Trainer Wii allows you to have your own personal training program where you can keep track of your progress. Throughout the game, you will be given valuable tips to help you master every challenge.     You can play Think! Logic Trainer Wii alone or use the Multiplayer Mode to compete against the whole family. Multiplayer mode allows you to compete with up to 16 players in 3 different modes: Tournament - Competition and Endurance... Make it fun and find out who has the highest "Think" quotient
