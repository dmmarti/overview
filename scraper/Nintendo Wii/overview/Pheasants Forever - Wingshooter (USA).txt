In Pheasants Forever, players have over 20 different types of wild game to hunt, including pheasants, duck, quail &amp; turkey. Pick your favorite breed of hunting dog to be your companion and set out on the hunt either alone or head-to-head with a friend!nnnnIn this game you hunt more than just pheasants. It has different stages for the hunter as you progress through the game and it has several different areas you hunt within. It can be easy to quite difficult depending on the area you hunt or the stage you have reached.
