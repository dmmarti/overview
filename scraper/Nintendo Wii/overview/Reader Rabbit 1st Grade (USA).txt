Why is raingear raining down on Wordville?   Journey with Reader Rabbit and Sam the Lion up to Cloud Nine to sort out this meteorlogical mystery!    Features:  Educational mini-games designed to teach basic 1st grade skills including Language Arts, Science, Problem Solving, and Math as well as Creative Exploration and Expression.  Multiple difficulty levels for each mini-game that can be changed at any time.  Full video cutscenes and voice acting throughout the game.  Simple controls using the Wii Remote.
