Harness the power of a raging tornado and battle the forces of evil in an effort to save the world! Sinister forces have deceived players to believe that their destructive behavior will save an epic hero. In reality, they re working for evil. Players must take control of a dynamic tornado to tear through open environments, chew up the landscape and spit out everything in the way. The more destruction, the bigger the tornado grows. Work against time based, collection and point challenge, players search for hidden elements and enemies from another world. As the debris flies and the trail of destruction is left behind, players must battle bosses and defeat the forces of evil.
