Petz Sports is a sim game designed for 1 to 4 players and revolving around caring for a puppy. The puppy will grow throughout the game.    It contains:    18 Dog Breeds: Bernese Mountain Dog, Chihuahua, Golden Retriever, Rottweiler, Poodle, and Pug.  22 Tricks  36 Agility Courses  Dogs can be dressed up and unlock various hair coat types and items can be unlocked. Up to four friends can play, and by using Wiimotes dogs can be exchanged.
