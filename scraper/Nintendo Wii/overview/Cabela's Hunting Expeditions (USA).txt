Outdoor enthusiasts head to wide-open and exotic locations around the world as they track down and hunt trophy game in Cabela's Hunting Expeditions. Using the new Strategic View system, players can plan their hunts and freely travel on foot or in a vehicle through 15 vast environments in North America and Africa. The action pits hunters against wildlife like bighorn sheep and brown bears, and the animals are designed to mimic real-life behavior, from herd mentalities to fight-or-flight responses. Completing hunts and challenges lets gamers earn new upgrades and customize their firearms.
