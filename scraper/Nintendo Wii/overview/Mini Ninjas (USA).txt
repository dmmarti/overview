Join Hiro, the smallest hero on the biggest adventure, as he embarks on an epic and electrifying quest to restore harmony to a world on the brink of chaos. Use your skills and mighty Ninja training to battle a magical army of evil Samurai. Harness the power of your Kuji magic and utilise the special skills of your Mini Ninja friends as you travel through a perilous world to reach the final confrontation with the Evil Samurai Warlord in his Fortress of Doom.Features:* Play as Hiro and his five friends in their epic quest to defeat the Evil Samurai Warlord* Master furious Ninja skills* Collect spells and unleash ancient Kuji Magic* Possess all animals* Stop time* Control the powers of nature* Restore harmony to the world
