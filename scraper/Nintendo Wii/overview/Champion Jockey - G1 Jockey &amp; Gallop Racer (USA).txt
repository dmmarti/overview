Champion Jockey: G1 Jockey &amp; Gallop Racer is a 2011 horse racing simulator video game from Tecmo Koei, released for the PlayStation 3, Wii and Xbox 360.    It is the spiritual successor to Koei G1 Jockey series and to Tecmo Gallop Racer series, making use of PlayStation Move and Kinect.
