Gravity is a fun filled puzzle game that will appeal to the young, the old, and everyone in between. The game has hours of game play and endless replayability with over 100 levels packed full of mind boggling and challenging puzzles. The player must master the correct placement of objects in order to advance to the next level. There is more than one way of completing a given puzzle - often times with surprising results. One thing that will stand out in this game is the high quality of hand painted background and artworks, as well as an atmospheric soundtrack.
