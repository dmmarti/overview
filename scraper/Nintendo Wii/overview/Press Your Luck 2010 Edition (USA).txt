No Whammys! No Whammys! And....stop! It s time to Press Your Luck as the classic game show makes its video game debut. Answer trivia questions correctly to earn spins on the big board, a shifting collection of big money and big prizes. Hit the button to stop on the square with the biggest bucks. Just hope you don t land on any Whammys, the little red critters who taunt you as they take away all your money and prizes. The game s loaded with 3,000 questions and 50 wacky whammy animations to keep things fresh. Customize your avatar, and experience all the fun of one of TV s old-school favorites.
