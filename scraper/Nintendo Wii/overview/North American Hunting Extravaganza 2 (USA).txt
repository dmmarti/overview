The All-In-One Hunting Experience Head out into the wild once again to track and hunt the most dangerous game in North America. Become a master hunter by traveling across the country and honing your skills to go after bigger and more dangerous prey. Set your sights on over 30 different wildlife species, with a variety weapons and hunting equipment at your disposal. Hunt alone or invite your friends to compete in a variety of mini-game challenges such as fowl hunting, target shooting, and more!
