Just Dance: Best Of (known as Just Dance: Greatest Hits) is a music video game released on the Wii and Xbox 360. It is part of the Just Dance video game series published by Ubisoft originally on the Wii. Just Dance: Greatest Hits includes songs from Just Dance, Just Dance 2, Just Dance: Summer Party, and Just Dance 3, with 25 songs in the PAL version (Best Of) and 35 songs in the NTSC version (Greatest Hits).
