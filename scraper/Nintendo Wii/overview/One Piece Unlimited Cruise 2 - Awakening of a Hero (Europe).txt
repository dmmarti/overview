One Piece Unlimited Cruise 2: Awakening of a Hero is a video game for the Nintendo Wii based on the manga/anime series One Piece. Episode 1 was released in Japan on September 11, 2008, with the second and last episode released February 26, 2009. Episode 2 have been released in Europe on June 19, 2009 and September 25, 2009 respectively.
