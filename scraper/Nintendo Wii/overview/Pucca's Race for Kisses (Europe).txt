Pucca has one great dream in her life: to kiss her sweet and tender Garu, the little Ninja, who does not have the slightest desire in the world to do so! But Pucca is a young lady who is as much in love as she is stubborn in her pursuit of Garu, and she is truly determined to cover him with kisses.     A fun-filled and interactive cartoon, Pucca s kisses game, will enable you, using the Wii Remote controller, to pause the episode whenever Pucca faces an obstacle or is in danger. Help her overcome it before heading out once again in pursuit of her loved one, Garu. At the end of each sprint, Garu must be found again, as he attempts, for the last time, to hide before being covered in kisses!
