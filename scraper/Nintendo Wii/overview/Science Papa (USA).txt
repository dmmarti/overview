Science Papa is the world s most renowned scientist. You ve just joined his elite team, and it s time for you to prove your worth! Explore chemistry, physics, paleontology, and biology in over 30 different experiments. You ll be competing against rival scientists who are out to steal prizes from under your nose. There are records to beat, lab equipment to unlock, and people to impress! Mix chemicals, heat solutions, unearth dinosaur bones, and rewire electronic gadgets, all using the innovative control scheme. Take on the challenge of the Solo Mode, or attempt to sabotage your friends and family as you race to successfully complete different experiments!
