World Tour Racing is an F1-style racing game for the Jaguar CD system.  You guide a very fast car on road courses based on countries around the world.    The graphics in World Tour Racing feature fully 3D environments constructed with texture-mapped polygons.  The various game modes include a championship, single race, and arcade mode.  Championship mode consists of a 16 race season.  Single race is self-explanatory, and arcade mode is similar to championship mode, except that advancement to the next race is based whether or not you finish high enough in each race..
